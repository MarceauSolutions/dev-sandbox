# Product Opportunity Log

*Weekly Review: Fridays 4 PM (via time-blocks)*
*Version: 2026-01-15*

## How to Use

When building a workflow, SOP, or automation, ask: **"Could someone pay for this?"**

If YES:
1. Add entry below in **Pending Review** section
2. Continue with your work (don't stop to analyze)
3. Review weekly on Friday 4 PM

## Decision Criteria

| Factor | Low Potential | High Potential |
|--------|---------------|----------------|
| **External Need** | Only useful internally | Others have same problem |
| **Effort to Productize** | 20+ hours | <10 hours |
| **Revenue Model** | Unclear | Obvious (SaaS, per-use, one-time) |
| **Competition** | Saturated market | Gap in market |
