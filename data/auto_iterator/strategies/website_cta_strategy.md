# Website CTA Optimization Strategy

> Guides the AutoIterator when proposing website call-to-action variants.

## Domain Context

Marceau Solutions website (marceausolutions.com) serves as the primary landing page
for digital services clients. Key pages: homepage, pricing, contact.

- **Brand**: Dark + Gold (#C9963C on #333333)
- **Target audience**: Small business owners in Naples/Fort Myers who need websites, automation, or AI tools
- **Primary conversion**: Contact form submission or Calendly booking
- **Secondary conversion**: Phone call to (239) 398-5676

## Current CTAs

| Page | CTA Text | Type |
|------|----------|------|
| Homepage hero | "Get a Free Consultation" | Button → contact page |
| Homepage services | "Learn More" | Link → service details |
| Pricing | "Get Started" | Button → contact form |
| Contact | "Send Message" | Form submit button |

## CTA Optimization Principles

1. **Specificity wins**: "Get Your Free Website Audit" > "Learn More"
2. **Value-first framing**: What they GET, not what they DO
3. **Urgency without fake scarcity**: "This week only" → bad. "Limited availability" → acceptable if true.
4. **Match visitor intent**: Homepage visitors are exploring; pricing visitors are evaluating
5. **Action + benefit**: "Book a Free Strategy Call — See Your Growth Plan" > "Contact Us"

## Brand Constraints

- **Voice**: Professional, direct, confident. No hype, no "revolutionary."
- **Words to use**: "Custom", "evidence-based", "results-driven", "local", "Naples"
- **Words to avoid**: "Cheap", "discount", "limited time offer", "act now", ALL CAPS
- **Color**: CTAs must be gold (#C9963C) on dark background
- **Mobile**: CTAs must work at ≤44px tap target

## Metrics

- **Clarity** (0-10): Is the action crystal clear?
- **Urgency** (0-10): Does it motivate immediate action?
- **Relevance** (0-10): Does it match the visitor's intent?
- **Value proposition** (0-10): Does it communicate what the visitor gets?

## GA4 Integration (Future)

When GA4 Measurement ID is activated:

## Variant Guidelines

- Change ONE element: text, color, position, or surrounding copy
- Always include a clear next step (what happens after click)
- Test benefit-oriented vs action-oriented CTAs
- Consider page context (homepage = awareness, pricing = decision)
