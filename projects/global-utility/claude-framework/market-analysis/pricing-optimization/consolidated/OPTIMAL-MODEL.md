## Sales Process

### Funnel

```
Free Content (YouTube, X, Proof-of-Concept demos)
              ↓
     Discovery Call (qualify, scope, price)
              ↓
     Proposal ($2,997 - $14,997 setup)
              ↓
     50% Upfront Payment
              ↓
     Delivery (2-4 weeks)
              ↓
     50% Final Payment
              ↓
     Retainer Offer ($747 or $2,247/mo)
```

### Scoping Questions (Determine Tier)

1. How many systems need to connect? (2-3 = Starter, 4-6 = Growth, 6+ = Enterprise)
2. Is AI/LLM processing required? (Yes = Growth minimum)
3. How many hours/week does this process take? (Calculate ROI)
4. What's the cost of errors today? (Justify premium)
5. Timeline urgency? (Rush = higher tier)
