## Recommended Strategy

### Structure: Parent Brand + Niche Subfolders

```
marceausolutions.com (Parent - AI Automation Agency)
├── /developers (PRIMARY - Claude implementations)
├── /fitness (SECONDARY - Creator automation)
├── /contractors (TERTIARY - Trade business automation)
└── /community (Claude Framework community)
```

### Why This Wins

1. **Consolidates SEO** - Subfolders inherit domain authority
2. **Niche messaging** - Each page speaks directly to that audience
3. **Flexibility** - Easy to add/remove verticals
4. **AI/Claude as anchor** - Strongest credibility, best price fit
5. **Serves all industries** - Without diluting positioning

## Niche Landing Pages

### /developers (PRIMARY)

**Headline:** "Claude Implementations That Actually Ship"

**Target:** Tech-forward SMBs, indie hackers, developers

**Proof Points:**
- 5 MCPs on Claude Registry
- 17 SOPs in Claude Framework
- GitHub repositories (inspectable code)
- PyPI packages

**Why Primary:**
- Strongest technical credibility
- Pricing justified ($3K-$15K normal for AI consulting)
- Clear differentiation from Nick Saraev (implement vs educate)
- MCP ecosystem growing exponentially

---

### /fitness (SECONDARY)

**Headline:** "AI Automation for Fitness Creators"

**Target:** Fitness influencers with 100K+ followers, earning $10K+/month

**Proof Points:**
- Fitness Influencer MCP (live, published)
- Content scheduling, video editing automation
- Revenue tracking

**Why Secondary:**
- Only top 5% of creators can afford $3K+ pricing
- Requires content marketing investment
- Market exists but smaller qualified pool

**Adjustment:** Consider $997 "Quickstart" tier for smaller creators

---

### /contractors (TERTIARY)

**Headline:** "AI for HVAC & Trade Contractors"

**Target:** HVAC distributors, trade businesses, service companies

**Proof Points:**
- HVAC Quotes MCP
- Lead Scraper for local business outreach

**Why Tertiary:**
- Brick-and-mortar sales cycle longer
- Less tech-savvy (requires more hand-holding)
- Good for case studies, not primary lead source initially

---

### /community

**Headline:** "The Claude Framework Community"

**Target:** Developers learning Claude/AI development

**Products:**
- Community Monthly ($47/mo)
- Community Annual ($397/yr)

**Purpose:** Brand building, warm lead generation for services
