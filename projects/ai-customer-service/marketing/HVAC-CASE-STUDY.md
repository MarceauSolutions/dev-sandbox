## Key Features That Drive Results

### 1. Natural Conversation Flow
**Sample AI Conversation:**

**Customer:** "Hi, I need someone to look at my AC. It's making a weird noise."

**AI:** "I can definitely help you with that. My name is Alex, and I work with SW Florida Comfort HVAC. Can I get your name and address?"

**Customer:** "Sure, it's John Smith, 123 Main Street in Naples."

**AI:** "Thanks, John. Just to confirm, you're hearing unusual noises from your AC unit. Is it an emergency, or can we schedule you for our next available appointment?"

**Customer:** "It's not an emergency, but I'd like someone out this week if possible."

**AI:** "Perfect. I have availability tomorrow at 2 PM or Thursday at 10 AM. Which works better for you?"

**Customer:** "Thursday at 10 works great."

**AI:** "Excellent. I've booked you for Thursday, January 18th at 10 AM. You'll receive a confirmation text shortly with our technician's name. Is there anything else I can help you with today?"

---

### 2. Emergency Dispatch
- AI recognizes keywords: "no heat," "no AC," "flooding," "gas leak"
- Immediately transfers to on-call technician
- Captures customer info during transfer
- Logs emergency in CRM for tracking

### 3. Automatic Follow-Up
- Every call gets logged in ClickUp CRM
- AI sends SMS confirmation to customer
- Owner receives lead notification instantly
- Follow-up sequence starts automatically if no answer
