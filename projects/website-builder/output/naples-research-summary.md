# Naples, FL Website Builder Research Summary

## Research Objective
Find local Naples, Florida businesses on Facebook that don't have websites, research them via social media, and build websites suited to their needs.

## Research Findings

### Market Opportunity
Based on research conducted (including articles from Brandignity and local business directories):

1. **Many Naples businesses lack professional websites** - Service businesses in particular rely heavily on Facebook and word-of-mouth
2. **Naples Chamber of Commerce** lists ~19,304 businesses, many small/local operations
3. **High-demand service categories** in Southwest Florida:
   - Pressure washing (critical due to Florida humidity/mold)
   - Lawn care & landscaping
   - Pool services (high pool ownership in Naples)
   - Mobile detailing
   - Junk removal
   - Pet services
   - Cleaning services
   - Handyman services
   - Food trucks

### Common Characteristics of Facebook-Only Businesses
- Family-owned operations
- 1-3 employees typically
- Service area: Naples, Marco Island, Bonita Springs, Estero
- Pricing: Competitive local rates
- Marketing: Word-of-mouth + Facebook posts
- No professional branding or web presence

### Southwest Florida Market Specifics
- **Seasonal business** (snowbird season Oct-Apr is peak)
- **Weather challenges** - Humidity causes mold/algae growth
- **High-end clientele** in communities like Grey Oaks, Pelican Bay, Park Shore
- **Property management demand** - Many rental/vacation homes need regular service
