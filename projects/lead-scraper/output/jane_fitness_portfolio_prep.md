3. **Membership** - Pricing tiers, signup form
4. **About** - Story, trainers, facility photos
5. **Contact** - Map, hours, phone, email

### Recommended Features
- [ ] Online class booking (MindBody, Vagaro, or custom)
- [ ] Membership signup with payment (Stripe)
