# AI Automation Agency Pricing Research

**Date:** 2026-01-17
**Purpose:** Inform pricing model for custom AI automations (MCPs, workflows, integrations)
**Target Niches:** Fitness influencers, HVAC distributors, and transferable across domains

---

## Executive Summary

Based on research into Nick Saraev's model, industry benchmarks, and value-based pricing strategies, the recommended positioning is:

- **Setup Fees:** $2,500 - $15,000 (based on complexity)
- **Monthly Retainers:** $500 - $3,000 (based on support level)
- **Positioning:** Premium niche specialist, not commodity SaaS
- **Value Capture:** 20-30% of client's annual savings/revenue impact

### His Pricing Framework

**Fixed Project Pricing:**
- Standard automations: $1,500 - $5,000 USD
- Complex systems (hiring, newsletter automation): $2,000 - $10,000 USD
- High-value implementations: $9,500+ per deal

**Monthly Maintenance:**
- Maintenance/support retainer: $500 - $1,000/month
- Fixed hours retainer for tweaks
- Upsell path into ongoing retainer product

**Value-Based Pricing Rule:**
> "Price at 30% of the cost saved and get 50% payment upfront"

This means a $7,000 project should deliver at least $20-25k in annual value to clients.

### Key Positioning Insights

1. **High-value implementation over templates** - A $9,500 deal is far more profitable than selling $350 templates
2. **SMB focus** - Faster feedback loops than chasing $500k enterprise deals
3. **Templateability = margins** - The more reusable the automation, the higher the leverage
4. **Three components of high-ROI automation:**
   - Clear deliverable
   - Hot-button pain point that impacts revenue
   - Templateable (reusable across clients)

## 3. Value-Based Pricing Framework

### ROI Calculation Formula

```
Client Value = (Hours Saved/Month x Hourly Rate x 12) + (Revenue Protected/Generated)

Your Price = Client Value x 0.20 to 0.30 (capture 20-30% of value)
```

**Example:**
- Client saves 20 hours/month at $60/hour = $14,400/year
- Plus: Captures 5 additional leads/month at $500 LTV = $30,000/year
- Total Client Value: $44,400/year
- Your Setup Fee: $8,880 - $13,320 (20-30%)
- Monthly Retainer: $740 - $1,110 (20-30% of monthly value)

### Time-Value Conversion Table

| Hours Saved/Month | At $50/hr | At $75/hr | At $100/hr |
|-------------------|-----------|-----------|------------|
| 10 hours | $6,000/yr | $9,000/yr | $12,000/yr |
| 20 hours | $12,000/yr | $18,000/yr | $24,000/yr |
| 40 hours | $24,000/yr | $36,000/yr | $48,000/yr |
| 80 hours | $48,000/yr | $72,000/yr | $96,000/yr |

**At 25% value capture:**
- 10 hrs saved @ $75/hr = $2,250 setup fee justified
- 40 hrs saved @ $75/hr = $9,000 setup fee justified

### Complexity Multipliers

| Factor | Multiplier | Rationale |
|--------|------------|-----------|
| Standard integrations (Zapier, Make) | 1.0x | Well-documented, predictable |
| Custom API work | 1.3x | Research, testing, edge cases |
| AI/LLM integration | 1.5x | Prompt engineering, tuning, unpredictability |
| Legacy system integration | 1.5-2.0x | Documentation gaps, workarounds |
| Multi-agent orchestration | 2.0x | Complex coordination, debugging |

### Industry-Specific Premiums

| Industry | Premium | Justification |
|----------|---------|---------------|
| Healthcare/HIPAA | +30-50% | Compliance, security requirements |
| Financial Services | +30-50% | Compliance, audit trails |
| Legal | +20-30% | Confidentiality, precision requirements |
| Fitness/Influencer | Standard | Digital-first, simpler compliance |
| HVAC/Service | +10-20% | Brick-and-mortar, legacy systems |
| E-commerce | Standard | Well-understood, good APIs |

**Note:** Fitness influencer niche aligns with Nick Saraev's ideal criteria:
- Digital-first (not brick-and-mortar)
- Mid-ticket+ (influencers invest in tools)
- Minimal regulations (no HIPAA/legal concerns)

## 4. Recommended Pricing Model

### Setup Fee Tiers

| Tier | Price | Scope | Hours (Est.) | Integrations |
|------|-------|-------|--------------|--------------|
| **Starter** | $2,500 - $5,000 | Single workflow, template-based | 10-20 hrs | 2-3 |
| **Growth** | $5,000 - $10,000 | Multi-step automation, some custom | 20-40 hrs | 4-6 |
| **Enterprise** | $10,000 - $15,000+ | Custom AI agent, complex orchestration | 40-80 hrs | 6+ |

### Monthly Retainer Tiers

| Tier | Price | Includes |
|------|-------|----------|
| **Maintenance** | $500/month | Bug fixes, minor tweaks, platform updates |
| **Support** | $1,000/month | + Up to 5 hours optimization, priority response |
| **Partner** | $2,000 - $3,000/month | + Proactive optimization, new feature builds, strategy calls |

### Scoping Questions (Determine Tier)

**Complexity Assessment:**
1. How many distinct systems need to connect? (2-3 = Starter, 4-6 = Growth, 6+ = Enterprise)
2. Do any require custom API work vs. native integrations?
3. Is AI/LLM processing involved?
4. Are there compliance/security requirements?
5. How much business logic/decision branching?

**Value Assessment:**
1. How many hours/week does this process currently take?
2. What's the hourly cost of the person doing it?
3. What revenue is at risk without this automation?
4. What's the cost of errors in the current process?

**Urgency Assessment:**
1. How soon do they need this?
2. What's the competitive pressure?
3. Is there a specific event/deadline driving this?

### Pricing Formula

```
Setup Fee = Base Tier + (Complexity Multiplier x $1,000) + Industry Premium

Monthly Retainer = Support Tier + (Integration Count x $100)
```

**Example: Fitness Influencer Video Automation**
- Base: Growth Tier ($7,500)
- Complexity: AI processing (1.5x) = +$1,500
- Industry: Standard (no premium)
- **Setup Fee: $9,000**
- Retainer: Support ($1,000) + 5 integrations ($500)
- **Monthly: $1,500**

## 6. Pricing Presentation Strategy

### Discovery Call Framework

1. **Understand the pain** (5 min)
   - "Walk me through your current process"
   - "How much time does this take?"
   - "What happens when it goes wrong?"

2. **Quantify the impact** (5 min)
   - "How many hours per week?"
   - "What's the cost of those hours?"
   - "What revenue depends on this?"

3. **Present value calculation** (3 min)
   - Show them their annual cost
   - Show potential savings
   - "If we could save you X hours, that's $Y/year"

4. **Position solution** (5 min)
   - "Based on what you've shared, this is a [Tier] project"
   - "Setup would be $X, which you'd recoup in Y months"
   - "Ongoing support ensures it keeps working"

### Objection Handling

| Objection | Response |
|-----------|----------|
| "That's expensive" | "Let's look at the ROI - you're spending $X/year on this process. At $Y, you break even in Z months." |
| "Can't you do it for less?" | "I can scope a smaller version - which parts are most critical?" |
| "Why not just use Zapier?" | "You could, but you'd spend 20+ hours figuring it out, then maintain it yourself. My clients prefer to stay focused on their business." |
| "I'll think about it" | "Totally understand. What information would help you decide? Want me to run the numbers against a different scenario?" |

### Payment Terms

**Setup:**
- 50% upfront (per Nick Saraev)
- 50% on delivery/acceptance
- Net 7 days

**Retainer:**
- Monthly, billed at start of month
- 3-month minimum commitment (optional)
- Cancel with 30 days notice
