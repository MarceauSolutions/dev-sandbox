# Website/Branding Optimization - SOP 9 Exploration

**Date:** 2026-01-17
**Purpose:** Determine optimal branding strategy for Marceau Solutions
**Method:** Multi-agent parallel exploration (SOP 9)

---

## Context

**Current State:**
- Website: marceausolutions.com (assumed generalist positioning)
- Projects: Fitness Influencer, HVAC Quotes, Lead Scraper, Claude Framework
- Pricing: Hybrid model ($2,997-$14,997 setup + $747-$2,247/mo retainer)
- Target: SMBs needing AI/automation implementations

**Key Tension:**
- Niching down = stronger positioning, easier marketing, higher perceived expertise
- But our projects are transferable across domains (fitness automation works for any creator)
- Nick Saraev niches for BRANDING but serves multiple industries

**Question to Answer:**
Should we rebrand/niche the website, or keep generalist with niche-specific landing pages?

---

## Evaluation Criteria

| Criterion | Weight | Description |
|-----------|--------|-------------|
| **Trust/Authority** | 25% | Does positioning build credibility? |
| **Lead Generation** | 25% | Will this attract qualified buyers? |
| **Flexibility** | 20% | Can we serve multiple industries? |
| **Marketing Efficiency** | 15% | Content/ad targeting clarity |
| **Implementation Cost** | 15% | Time/money to execute |

---

## Four Approaches to Evaluate

### Agent 1: Generalist (Status Quo)
- "Marceau Solutions - AI Automation Agency"
- Serve all industries from one brand
- Use case studies to show breadth
- Risk: Jack of all trades, master of none

### Agent 2: Niche - Fitness/Creator Economy
- "Marceau Solutions - AI for Fitness Creators"
- Lead with Fitness Influencer MCP as hero product
- All content focused on creator economy
- Other industries served quietly

### Agent 3: Niche - AI Automation (Claude-focused)
- "Marceau Solutions - Claude Implementation Experts"
- Lead with Claude Framework, technical positioning
- Target developers and tech-forward SMBs
- Nick Saraev-adjacent positioning

### Agent 4: Multi-Brand / Landing Page Strategy
- Keep Marceau Solutions as parent
- Create niche landing pages/micro-sites:
  - /fitness - Fitness creators
  - /hvac - Trade contractors
  - /developers - Claude Framework
- SEO + paid ads to niche pages

---

## Research Questions Per Agent

1. What's the homepage headline and positioning statement?
2. What proof/case studies lead the page?
3. How do we handle inquiries from "wrong" niche?
4. What's the content marketing strategy?
5. What's the SEO/paid ads approach?
6. How does this align with $2,997+ pricing?
