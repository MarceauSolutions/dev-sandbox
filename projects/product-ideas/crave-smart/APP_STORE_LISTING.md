## App Description (4000 characters max)

**Stop guessing what she wants for dinner. Start knowing.**

Ever heard "I don't know, you pick" only to have every suggestion rejected? You're not alone. And it's not random—it's science.

**91.78% of women experience predictable food cravings tied to their menstrual cycle.**

CraveSmart uses this research to predict what she'll crave, before she knows herself.

### How It Works

CraveSmart connects to Apple Health to read cycle data (from apps like Flo, Clue, or Apple's built-in tracker). Using peer-reviewed research on hormonal cravings, it predicts:

• **Day 1-5 (Menstrual):** Iron-rich foods, warm comfort meals
• **Day 6-13 (Follicular):** Fresh, light, energizing options
• **Day 14-16 (Ovulation):** Social dining, adventurous choices
• **Day 17-28 (Luteal):** Sweet, salty, carbs—the chocolate phase

### Key Features

**Daily Predictions**
Open the app each morning to see today's craving forecast with confidence scores.

**Pattern Learning**
Log meals to help CraveSmart learn her personal patterns. "Thai food on Thursdays" becomes a prediction.

**Partner Sharing (Premium)**
Share predictions with your partner so they always know what to suggest.

**Privacy First**
All data stays on your device. We never see or store your health information.

**Science-Backed**
Based on peer-reviewed research from PMC and leading nutrition science journals.

### Notes for Reviewer
CraveSmart reads menstrual cycle data from Apple Health to predict food cravings. This is based on peer-reviewed scientific research showing that hormonal changes during the menstrual cycle affect food preferences.

The app requires HealthKit access to read menstrual flow data. If no HealthKit data is available, users can manually enter their cycle information.

No account is required for basic functionality. Premium features (partner sharing) require an in-app purchase subscription.
