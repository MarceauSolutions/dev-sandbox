# Market Viability Analysis Plan: Elder Tech Concierge

**Created:** 2026-01-16
**Status:** COMPLETE

---

## Product Hypothesis

**What it is:** An in-home service that sets up personalized AI assistants (Claude-based) for elderly people and those who struggle with technology.

**Who it's for:**
- Primary: 65+ seniors who struggle with technology
- Secondary: 55-65 adults needing tech assistance
- Tertiary: Adult children wanting to help aging parents

**Problem it solves:** Seniors are left behind by technology, leading to:
- Social isolation (can't video call grandkids)
- Safety risks (scam vulnerability)
- Health impacts (missed medications, appointments)
- Family burden (constant tech support calls)

**How we'd make money:**
- One-time setup fee ($299-499)
- Monthly subscription for support ($49-99/month)

## Research Questions by Agent

### Agent 1: Market Size

- [x] What is the TAM (Total Addressable Market)?
- [x] What is the SAM (Serviceable Addressable Market)?
- [x] What is the realistic SOM (Serviceable Obtainable Market)?
- [x] Is the market growing, stable, or shrinking?
- [x] What are the key market trends?

**Findings:**
- TAM: $13B (26M tech-struggling seniors x $500/year)
- SAM: $7.8B (30% income-qualified)
- SOM: $50K-150K Year 1, $600K-1.2M Year 3 (local/regional)
- Growth: Strong (10,000 turning 65 daily through 2030)
- Score: 5/5

### Agent 2: Competition

- [x] Who are the direct competitors (same solution)?
- [x] Who are indirect competitors (different solution, same problem)?
- [x] What do competitors charge? What's their revenue model?
- [x] What's their market share / funding / size?
- [x] What's their main weakness we could exploit?
- [x] Is there a gap in the market?

**Findings:**
- Direct: Geek Squad ($100-200/visit), HelloTech ($79-149), Candoo Tech ($50-80/hr)
- Indirect: Family members, YouTube tutorials, phone carrier stores
- Gap: No player combines in-home + AI-focused + senior-specialized + ongoing support
- Score: 4/5

### Agent 3: Customer Research

- [x] Who exactly is the target customer (be specific)?
- [x] How painful is this problem (1-10)?
- [x] How do they currently solve it (workarounds)?
- [x] How much do they currently pay for alternatives?
- [x] Where do they congregate online (forums, social)?
- [x] What triggers them to search for a solution?

**Findings:**
- Target: 68-75 year olds with out-of-town adult children
- Pain level: 8-10/10 (safety, health, connection at stake)
- Current solutions: Overburdened family, avoidance, unused devices
- Acquisition channels: Senior centers, geriatricians, churches, Facebook (adult children)
- Score: 5/5

### Agent 4: Monetization

- [x] What pricing model makes sense (subscription, usage, one-time)?
- [x] What price point would the market bear?
- [x] What are realistic customer acquisition costs?
- [x] What's the expected LTV (lifetime value)?
- [x] What's the break-even timeline?
- [x] Are there adjacent revenue opportunities (upsells)?

**Findings:**
- Model: Setup fee + monthly subscription
- Pricing: $299-499 setup, $49-99/month
- CAC: $50-100 (referral-heavy)
- LTV: $477-1,027 (12-month)
- LTV:CAC: 10x+
- Break-even: 10 customers covers monthly costs
- Score: 4/5
