Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Summary: CraveSmart stores all your data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

1. Introduction

CraveSmart ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

CraveSmart is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do NOT:

  • Collect or transmit your health data to any server
  • Store your menstrual cycle information on our servers
  • Track your location
  • Sell or share any personal information
  • Use third-party analytics that collect personal data
  • Create user accounts that store data remotely

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following information is stored only on your device and never leaves it:

  • Cycle Data: Information read from Apple Health about your menstrual cycle
  • Food Logs: Meals and cravings you choose to log
  • Predictions: Craving predictions generated by the app
  • Preferences: Your app settings and preferences
  • Patterns: Learned patterns about your food preferences

4. Apple HealthKit Integration

CraveSmart requests access to read menstrual cycle data from Apple HealthKit. This data:

  • Is read directly from HealthKit on your device
  • Is processed entirely on your device
  • Is never transmitted to our servers or any third party
  • Can be revoked at any time in your iPhone's Settings

To manage HealthKit permissions: Settings → Privacy & Security → Health → CraveSmart

5. Partner Sharing Feature (Premium)

If you choose to use the Partner Sharing feature:

  • You explicitly opt-in to share specific predictions
  • Data is shared directly between devices using end-to-end encryption
  • You can revoke sharing at any time
  • We cannot access the shared data

6. In-App Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed entirely by Apple. We do not receive or store any payment information. Apple's privacy policy applies to these transactions.

7. Children's Privacy

CraveSmart is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Deleting the app removes all CraveSmart data
  • You can clear app data in your iPhone's Settings
  • No data remains on any server because we don't store any

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
