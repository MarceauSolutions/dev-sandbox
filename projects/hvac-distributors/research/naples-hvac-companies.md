## Key Findings

### Market Characteristics
1. **Service call fees** range from $29 (Coolair promo) to $125+ (Gulfstar)
2. **Most companies** offer free estimates on new equipment
3. **24/7 availability** is standard among major providers
4. **Maintenance plans** typically offer 10-15% discounts on repairs
5. **Family-owned businesses** dominate the Naples market

### Competitive Pricing Strategies
- Coolair's $29 service call is significantly below market
- Naples Air Conditioning's $95/hr with no service fee is transparent
- Many companies waive diagnostic fees when repairs are completed
- Veterans and seniors often receive special discounts

### Certification Patterns
- **Trane Comfort Specialist**: JP Brett, Certified Heating, Key to Cool
- **NATE Certified**: Conditioned Air, Certified Heating, Key to Cool
- **Lennox Dealer**: Cool Zone, Gulf Shore Cooling
- **Rheem Pro Partner**: Coolair
- **Mitsubishi Diamond**: Gulf Shore Cooling
