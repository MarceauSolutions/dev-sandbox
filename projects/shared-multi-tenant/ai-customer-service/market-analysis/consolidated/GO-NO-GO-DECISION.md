## Rationale

### Why GO

This opportunity scores above the 4.0 threshold across all critical dimensions:

1. **Exceptional Customer Pain (9/10)**
   - 70% of restaurant operators have unfilled positions
   - 80%+ annual turnover creates perpetual staffing crisis
   - Owners working entry-level positions during peak hours
   - This is an **existential problem**, not a nice-to-have

2. **Clear Willingness to Pay**
   - Restaurants already spending $26K+/year on phone staff
   - AI solutions at $2,400-6,000/year = 77-91% savings
   - 760% ROI documented in case studies
   - Budget exists, just needs reallocation

3. **Large Growing Market**
   - TAM: $50B+ by 2030 (23-32% CAGR)
   - SAM: $2.25B in US restaurant voice AI
   - SOM: $1.5M ARR by Year 3 (conservative)

4. **Defensible Position Available**
   - SMB segment ($199-349/mo) ignored by enterprise players
   - Phone order niche easier than drive-thru (lower accuracy bar)
   - Video avatar = blue ocean differentiation
   - Vertical integration with other Marceau services

5. **Strong Unit Economics**
   - LTV:CAC ratio: 5-7:1 (minimum viable is 3:1)
   - CAC payback: 2-3 months (target is <12)
   - Break-even: 12-18 months with 80 customers
   - Gross margin: 70-78%

6. **Perfect Timing**
   - 2026 called "year of AI-driven restaurant"
   - Major chains (Wendy's, Taco Bell, Pizza Hut) validating category
   - McDonald's failure created pause = opportunity for "proven" solutions
   - Operators' "breaking point" reached in 2025

### Known Risks (Manageable)

| Risk | Severity | Mitigation |
|------|----------|------------|
| **$700M competitor funding** | High | They focus on enterprise; we target SMB |
| **High SMB churn (3-7%/mo)** | Medium | Invest in customer success early |
| **AI accuracy concerns** | Medium | Target 90%+; phone is easier than drive-thru |
| **Integration friction** | Medium | Pre-integrate Toast/Square before launch |

## Alternative Considered: NO-GO

If we chose NO-GO, the reasons would be:

1. **$700M in competitor funding** - Would require significant capital to compete
2. **High SMB churn** - Customer retention is expensive
3. **AI accuracy not there yet** - McDonald's failed at 80%

**Why we reject NO-GO**: These risks are manageable. Competitors aren't targeting SMB. Churn can be managed with customer success. Phone orders are easier than drive-thru (lower accuracy bar).
