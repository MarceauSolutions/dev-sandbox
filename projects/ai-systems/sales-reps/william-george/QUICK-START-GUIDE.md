## What We Sell

**An AI phone agent that actually answers calls and talks to customers** — not voicemail, not text-back, a real conversation.

What it does:
- **Picks up the phone 24/7** — 9pm emergency? AI answers live.
- **Has a real conversation** — qualifies the lead, asks questions, handles objections
- **Books appointments on the spot** — customer hangs up with a confirmed time
- **Texts you instantly** — when it's a hot lead or urgent job
- **Handles FAQs** — pricing, hours, services, directions

**In plain English:** "An AI employee that never sleeps, never calls in sick, and books jobs while you're at dinner."

**This is NOT another auto-text system.** Most businesses already have that. This is an actual voice AI that talks to customers like a receptionist would.
