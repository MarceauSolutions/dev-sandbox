## Rationale

Elder Tech Concierge addresses a perfect storm of favorable market conditions:

### Why This Works

1. **Demographic Inevitability:** 10,000 Americans turn 65 every day. This market grows automatically for the next 20 years regardless of marketing efforts. You're not creating demand; you're capturing existing demand.

2. **Pain is Severe and Emotional:** This isn't a "nice to have" - seniors face real risks (scams costing billions annually), real isolation (loneliness epidemic), and real health impacts (missed medications). Family members carry guilt and worry. These emotions drive purchasing decisions.

3. **No One Owns This Niche:** Big players (Geek Squad) are too generic and intimidating. Senior-focused services haven't integrated AI. AI companies haven't focused on seniors. First-mover advantage is available.

4. **Unit Economics Work:** 70%+ gross margins on recurring revenue with 10x+ LTV:CAC ratio. This is a sustainable business model, not a money-losing growth play.

5. **Low Capital Requirements:** This is a service business. No inventory, no manufacturing, no complex technology development. Start with a tablet, Claude, and a car.

### Why This Could Fail

1. **Execution Risk:** In-home services are operationally complex. Need reliable, patient, trustworthy staff.

2. **Trust Building Takes Time:** Seniors don't adopt services quickly. Word-of-mouth is slow but sticky.

3. **Churn Reality:** Customer lifespan limited by mortality, cognitive decline, and care facility transitions.

4. **Regulatory Uncertainty:** AI regulation for vulnerable populations could create compliance burden.

## If GO (Recommended Path)

### Immediate Next Steps (Week 1-2)

1. **Form LLC** and get basic E&O insurance
2. **Set up Claude Desktop** with simplified interface concept
3. **Create setup checklist** and support documentation
4. **Identify 5-10 beta customers** from personal network

### MVP Validation (Month 1-3)

- **Offer free/discounted setups** to 10 seniors
- **Document everything**: What works, what doesn't, what they ask for
- **Collect testimonials** and referrals
- **Refine the product** based on real feedback

### Launch Criteria

Before charging full price:
- [ ] 10 successful beta setups completed
- [ ] At least 5 positive testimonials collected
- [ ] Setup process takes under 3 hours consistently
- [ ] Support requests manageable (< 2 hours/customer/month)
- [ ] At least 3 referrals generated organically

### Target Customer Profile

**Ideal First Customer:**
- Age 68-75 (young enough to engage, old enough to need help)
- Has smartphone but underuses it
- Adult children live out of town (motivated family payer)
- Middle/upper-middle income (can afford premium)
- Active in community (referral potential)
- Open to trying new things (early adopter mindset)

### Pricing Strategy

**Launch Pricing (validate willingness to pay):**
- Setup: $299 (discounted from $399)
- Monthly: $49
- "Founding customer" framing

**Mature Pricing (after 50 customers):**
- Essential: $299 setup + $49/month
- Premium: $499 setup + $99/month

### Go-to-Market Priorities

1. **Geriatrician partnerships** - Ask doctors to refer patients who struggle with medication/appointment compliance
2. **Senior center workshops** - Free "AI for Seniors" demo sessions
3. **Church/faith community** - Trust-based referral network
4. **Adult children targeting** - Facebook/Google ads: "Help your aging parent stay connected"

## Key Metrics to Track

### Leading Indicators
- Demo requests/week
- Beta customer satisfaction (NPS)
- Time to setup completion
- Support tickets per customer

### Lagging Indicators
- Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
- Customer acquisition cost (CAC)
- Churn rate (monthly)
- Referral rate (customers who refer)

### Decision Checkpoints

**Month 3 Review:**
- Are 8+ of 10 beta customers satisfied?
- Have we received 3+ organic referrals?
- Is setup process repeatable in < 3 hours?

If YES to all: Proceed to paid launch
If NO: Iterate or pivot

**Month 6 Review:**
- Do we have 30+ paying customers?
- Is CAC < $100?
- Is monthly churn < 5%?

If YES to all: Hire first technician
If NO: Reassess pricing/positioning

**Month 12 Review:**
- Have we hit $50K+ revenue?
- Is LTV:CAC > 5x?
- Do we have operational playbook?

If YES to all: Expand to second metro area
If NO: Evaluate whether to continue

## Risk Mitigation Plan

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Seniors reject AI | Medium | High | Lead with "assistant" framing, not "AI" |
| High churn | High | Medium | Target younger seniors, family payers |
| Competitor enters | Low | Medium | Build local brand, referral network |
| Claude costs increase | Low | Medium | Build margin buffer, adjust pricing |
| Regulatory changes | Low | High | Conservative data handling, stay informed |

**Proceed with Elder Tech Concierge.**
