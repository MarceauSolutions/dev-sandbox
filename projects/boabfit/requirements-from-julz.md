## 6 Retention Features That Make Fitness Apps Explode

### 1. Daily "Open the App" Reason
- "Day 8: Glutes + Core"
- Streak tracker (🔥 12-day streak)
- Daily challenge
- Progress reminder
- **Can double retention**

### 2. "Press Play" Workout Flow
Mindless experience:
1. Open app
2. Tap Start Workout
3. Timer + exercise auto-advance
4. Done

*Julz already does this with 30-minute BOAB workouts — perfect for app*

### 3. Progress Visuals (Addictive)
- Strength chart
- Weight chart
- Streak counter
- Before/after gallery
- "Your glutes workout volume increased 32% this month"

### 4. Community (HUGE)
- Group chat
- Weekly challenges
- Leaderboards
- Progress photo posting
- *BOABFIT girls community could live inside app*

Examples: Strava, Peloton work because people stay for the people.

### 5. Notifications That Pull Users Back
- "Julz just dropped today's glute workout 🍑"
- "Don't break your 8-day streak."
- "BOAB girls completed 312 workouts today."

### 6. Transformation Tracking
- Photo progress timeline
- Waist measurement tracker
- Body weight graph
- Workout history
