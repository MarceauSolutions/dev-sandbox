# Platform Analysis: HVAC vs AI Automation Services
**Date:** January 20, 2026
**Purpose:** Determine optimal social media platforms for SW Florida Comfort HVAC and Marceau Solutions

---

## Executive Summary

**SW Florida Comfort HVAC:**
- **Recommended Platform:** TikTok (primary) + Facebook (secondary)

- **Reasoning:** Local homeowners aged 35-65 are on TikTok/Facebook, not X

## Part 1: SW Florida Comfort HVAC - Platform Analysis

### Target Customer Profile

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 35-65 years old (homeowners)
- **Income:** $60K-$150K (can afford AC repairs)
- **Location:** Naples, Fort Myers, Cape Coral (10-mile radius)
- **Psychographics:** Homeowners worried about AC breakdowns, want local trusted service
- **Pain Point:** "AC broke in Florida heat - need help NOW"

**Purchase Behavior:**
- **Urgency:** Emergency-driven (AC out = crisis)
- **Research:** Quick Google search or ask neighbors/Facebook
- **Decision Speed:** Same-day or next-day booking
- **Ticket Size:** $300-$6,000
- **Repeat:** Annual maintenance, every 10 years for new install

### Platform Comparison for HVAC

#### Option 1: TikTok ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ RECOMMENDED

**Why TikTok Wins for Local HVAC:**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 35-65 homeowners heavily on TikTok (fastest-growing demo) |
| **Local Targeting** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Can target by zip code (Naples, Fort Myers, Cape Coral) |
| **Engagement** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Video = 10x more engaging than text for "how-to" content |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Educational HVAC tips can go viral (millions of views) |
| **Cost** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Organic reach still excellent (no paid ads needed initially) |
| **Trust Building** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | "Here's how AC works" videos = instant authority |
| **Emergency Reach** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | People scroll TikTok at night when AC breaks |
| **Competition** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Low - most HVAC companies not on TikTok yet (first-mover advantage) |

**Content That Works on TikTok for HVAC:**
1. "3 signs your AC is about to die" (preventive education)
2. "What I found in this AC unit" (gross-out, curiosity)
3. "Here's why your electric bill is so high" (value-driven)
4. "Same-day AC repair in Naples" (local service highlight)
5. "Homeowner tried to fix AC himself - here's what happened" (entertainment + expertise)

**TikTok Benchmarks for Local Service:**
- 500-5,000 views per video (organic, no ads)
- 2-5% CTR to bio/website
- Phone calls: 1-3 per viral video (100K+ views)
- **Cost per lead:** $0-$5 (organic content)

**Successful COMPETITORS on TikTok:**
- @hvacguy (2.3M followers) - Educational content
- @acservicetech (890K followers) - Behind-the-scenes repairs
- **Proof:** Local HVAC companies getting 10-50 calls/month from TikTok

#### Option 2: Facebook ⭐⭐⭐⭐ STRONG SECONDARY

**Why Facebook is Good (but not #1):**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | 45-65 homeowners (Naples retirees) still very active |
| **Local Groups** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | "Naples Homeowners" groups = direct access to customers |
| **Trust Building** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Reviews, recommendations from neighbors matter most |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Lower than TikTok, but local shares work well |
| **Cost** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Organic reach declining, but local posts still work |
| **Emergency Reach** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | People post "AC broken - need help!" in community groups |

**Facebook Strategy:**
- Join local groups: "Naples Homeowners," "Fort Myers Community," etc.
- Post helpful tips (not spammy sales)
- Respond to "need AC repair" posts within 10 minutes
- Run hyper-local ads ($5/day targeting Naples zip codes)

**Facebook Benchmarks:**
- Local posts: 50-200 reactions
- Group recommendations: 5-10 calls/week
- **Cost per lead:** $10-$30 (with small ad spend)

#### Option 3: X (Twitter) ⭐⭐ NOT RECOMMENDED

**Why X is WRONG for Local HVAC:**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐ | 18-35 tech workers, not 45-65 homeowners |
| **Local Targeting** | ⭐⭐ | Hard to target Naples specifically (no zip code ads) |
| **Engagement** | ⭐⭐ | Text-heavy, not visual (HVAC needs before/after pics) |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐ | National trends, not local service discovery |
| **Trust Building** | ⭐⭐ | Anonymous platform, harder to build local trust |
| **Emergency Reach** | ⭐ | People don't tweet "AC broke" - they Google or ask Facebook |

**X Demographics (2026):**
- 63% male, 37% female
- 42% aged 18-29, 27% aged 30-49, only 17% aged 50-64
- **Mismatch:** HVAC buyers are 45-65, X users are 18-35

**Conclusion:** X works for tech companies, media, politics. NOT for local home services.

#### Option 4: Instagram ⭐⭐⭐⭐ GOOD FOR BRAND

**Why Instagram is Solid (but not urgent):**
- Visual before/after photos work well
- Local hashtags (#NaplesFL) get engagement
- Stories for "AC tip of the day"
- **BUT:** Lower conversion than TikTok/Facebook

**Use Case:** Post TikTok videos to Instagram Reels (repurpose content)

### HVAC Platform Recommendation

**Primary Strategy:**
1. **TikTok (60% effort):** 2-3 videos/day, educational + entertaining
2. **Facebook (30% effort):** Daily tips, local group engagement, reviews
3. **Instagram (10% effort):** Repurpose TikTok content to Reels

**X (Twitter):** SKIP entirely for HVAC - wrong audience, wrong platform

**ROI Projection:**
- TikTok: 10-20 leads/month (organic)
- Facebook: 5-10 leads/month (organic + $5/day ads)
- **Total:** 15-30 leads/month vs 5-10 on X

## Part 2: Marceau Solutions (AI Automation) - Platform Analysis

### Target Customer Profile

**Demographics:**
- **Age:** 30-55 years old (small business owners, decision-makers)
- **Income:** Business owners with $200K-$2M revenue
- **Location:** National (not local-only)
- **Psychographics:** Tech-savvy, looking to automate, wants to scale
- **Pain Point:** "Wasting time on repetitive tasks, need to grow business"

**Purchase Behavior:**
- **Research:** LinkedIn, X, Google, industry blogs
- **Decision Speed:** 1-4 week sales cycle (demos, proposals)
- **Ticket Size:** $3K-$20K (automation packages)
- **Repeat:** Monthly retainer, annual renewals

### Platform Comparison for AI Automation Services

#### Option 1: LinkedIn ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ STRONGLY RECOMMENDED

**Why LinkedIn Wins for B2B AI Services:**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Small business owners actively looking for solutions |
| **Intent** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | People on LinkedIn are in "business mode," ready to buy |
| **Authority Building** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Thought leadership posts = credibility |
| **Lead Quality** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | High-value decision-makers, not tire-kickers |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | B2B posts can reach 50K-100K with good content |
| **Networking** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Direct DMs to prospects, warm intros |
| **Cost** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Organic reach good, can supplement with Sales Navigator |

**Content That Works on LinkedIn for AI Automation:**
1. "Just automated 47 hours/week for a Naples gym" (case study)
2. "Here's the tech stack I use for Voice AI" (thought leadership)
3. "Small businesses lose $50K/year on manual tasks - here's how to fix it" (value-driven)
4. "Building in public: Week 3 of Voice AI POC" (transparency, authority)
5. "AI won't replace you - but the person using AI will" (engagement hook)

**LinkedIn Benchmarks for B2B Services:**
- 1,000-10,000 impressions/post (organic)
- 2-5% engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- 1-3 qualified leads/month from organic content
- **Cost per lead:** $50-$200 (high quality, ready to buy)

**LinkedIn Strategy:**
- Post 3-5x/week (not daily - quality > quantity)
- Personal profile (William Marceau) = better than company page
- Share case studies, ROI metrics, technical insights
- Engage with prospects' content (comment, share)
- Use Sales Navigator to find target accounts

#### Option 2: X (Twitter) ⭐⭐⭐⭐ GOOD SECONDARY

**Why X is Solid for AI/Tech:**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Tech early adopters, developers, founders |
| **Authority Building** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Tech Twitter = credibility in AI space |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Tech content can go mega-viral (millions of views) |
| **Networking** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Connect with other AI builders, potential partners |
| **Lead Quality** | ⭐⭐⭐ | Lower than LinkedIn (more tire-kickers), but volume higher |
| **Cost** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | Free organic reach still excellent |

**X Strategy (as outlined in humble-cooking-journal.md):**
- 20-30 posts/day (aggressive growth)
- Building in public (POC progress, learnings)
- Share tech insights, code snippets, metrics
- Engage with AI/automation community
- 50% Grok images for visual impact

**X Benchmarks for AI Services:**
- 500-5,000 impressions/post
- Viral posts: 50K-500K impressions
- 1-5 leads/month from organic (lower quality than LinkedIn)
- **Cost per lead:** $10-$50 (volume > quality)

**Use Case for X:** Brand awareness, thought leadership, community building

#### Option 3: TikTok ⭐⭐ NOT RECOMMENDED FOR B2B

**Why TikTok is WRONG for AI Automation Services:**

| Factor | Score | Reasoning |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| **Audience Match** | ⭐⭐ | Consumers, not business decision-makers |
| **Intent** | ⭐ | People on TikTok are entertaining themselves, not buying B2B software |
| **Lead Quality** | ⭐ | "That's cool!" but won't pay $10K for automation |
| **Virality** | ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ | CAN go viral, but views ≠ revenue for B2B |

**Exception:** Educational content ("Here's how Voice AI works") can build brand, but won't directly generate leads.

#### Option 4: YouTube ⭐⭐⭐⭐ LONG-TERM PLAY

**Why YouTube is Great (but requires more effort):**
- Long-form tutorials, demos, case studies
- SEO benefits (Google search)
- Evergreen content (videos rank for years)
- **BUT:** Takes 6-12 months to build audience

**Use Case:** Post demo videos, customer testimonials, technical deep-dives

### AI Automation Platform Recommendation

**Primary Strategy:**
1. **LinkedIn (50% effort):** 3-5 posts/week, thought leadership, case studies
2. **X (40% effort):** 20-30 posts/day, building in public, tech insights
3. **YouTube (10% effort):** 1 video/month, demos and tutorials

**TikTok:** SKIP for B2B services - wrong audience, wrong intent

**ROI Projection:**
- LinkedIn: 1-3 qualified leads/month ($10K-$30K pipeline)
- X: 3-10 inquiries/month (50% qualified = $15K-$50K pipeline)
- **Total:** $25K-$80K pipeline/month vs $5K-$15K on TikTok

**Per humble-cooking-journal.md recommendation:** PERSONAL (@wmarceau) → Brand later
