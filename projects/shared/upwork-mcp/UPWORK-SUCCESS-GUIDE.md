## Job Qualification Framework

Based on [GigRadar's qualification system](https://gigradar.io/blog/how-to-qualify-upwork-jobs-in-60-seconds) and [client red flags research](https://gigradar.io/blog/icp-red-flags-for-upwork-agencies):

### 60-Second Job Screening

**GREEN FLAGS (Apply):**
- ✅ Payment verified
- ✅ History of hires (10+ jobs posted, 50%+ hire rate)
- ✅ Total spend $1,000+ (proves they pay)
- ✅ Average rating given: 4.5+ stars
- ✅ Clear scope with specific deliverables
- ✅ Budget matches your rate ($500+ for dashboards)
- ✅ Posted within last 24-48 hours
- ✅ <15 proposals already

**RED FLAGS (Skip):**
- ❌ Payment NOT verified
- ❌ No hire history (0 jobs completed)
- ❌ Avg hourly rate paid: <$15/hr (your rate is $73)
- ❌ Poor reviews from freelancers
- ❌ "Urgent" + vague scope = scope creep
- ❌ Asks for free test/sample work
- ❌ Mentions "AI content bypass" or similar
- ❌ Wants to communicate off-platform
- ❌ 50+ proposals already (too competitive)

### Quick Scoring Rubric

Rate each job 0-5 on:

| Factor | Score (0-5) |
|--------|-------------|
| **Fit & Proof** - Can you prove you can do this? | |
| **Scope Clarity** - Is the deliverable clear? | |
| **Value Alignment** - Does budget match scope? | |
| **Client Signals** - Green flags present? | |
| **TOTAL** | /20 |

**Decision:**
- 17-20: Apply immediately
- 14-16: Apply later today
- 13 or less: Skip

## Winning Proposal Template

Based on [Upwork's proposal guide](https://www.upwork.com/resources/how-to-create-a-proposal-that-wins-jobs) and [Career Sidekick templates](https://careersidekick.com/upwork-proposals/):

### Key Stats
- Average winning proposal: **93 words**
- First 2 sentences: **Most important** (all client sees initially)
- Response within 24 hours: **+35% win rate**

### Proposal Structure

```
[HOOK - Show you read the job post]
I noticed you're looking for [specific thing from job post].

[RELEVANCE - Connect your experience]
I recently built [similar thing] that [result/outcome].
[Optional: Link to portfolio item]

[SOLUTION - Brief approach]
For your project, I'd [1-2 sentence approach].

[CTA - Next step]
Happy to discuss specifics—what questions do you have?

William
```

### Example Proposal (Amazon Dashboard Job)

```
I saw you need a dashboard to track your FBA profitability across 15 SKUs.

I built a similar system for an Amazon seller doing $24K/month—it pulls
SP-API data automatically and calculates true profit after FBA fees,
PPC spend, and refunds. Here's a sample: [portfolio link]

For your project, I'd set up automated data pulls and a Google Sheets
dashboard you can check daily. Takes about a week.

Any specific metrics you want to prioritize?

William
```

**Word count: 78 words** (under the 93-word sweet spot)

### What NOT to Do

❌ "Dear Sir/Madam" - generic
❌ "I am a highly skilled professional" - about you, not them
❌ Copying their job post back to them
❌ Listing every skill you have
❌ "Please give me a chance" - desperate
❌ Wall of text with no spacing

## Core Service: Restock Decision Analysis

This is a **key differentiator** - most FBA "experts" don't offer quantitative decision support.

### The Problem Sellers Face

Every restock decision is a trade-off:

| Over-Order Risk | Under-Order Risk |
|-----------------|------------------|
| Higher storage fees (especially aged inventory surcharges) | Stockout = lose Buy Box |
| Capital tied up in slow-moving inventory | Lost sales during stockout |
| Long-term storage fees after 365 days | Competitor gains momentum |
| Potential liquidation losses | Algorithm penalizes inconsistent availability |

**Most sellers guess.** You build them a system to calculate the optimal order quantity.

### What You Build

**Restock Decision Calculator** that factors in:

1. **Current velocity** - Units sold per day (trailing 7, 30, 90 days)
2. **Lead time** - Days from order to Amazon receipt
3. **Storage costs** - Monthly storage fees by size tier + aged inventory surcharges
4. **Stockout cost** - Estimated lost profit per day without Buy Box
5. **Capital cost** - Cost of money tied up in inventory (opportunity cost)
6. **Seasonality** - Demand multipliers for upcoming periods

### The Output

```
SKU: Widget-XL-Blue
Current Stock: 150 units
Daily Velocity: 8.3 units/day
Days of Stock: 18 days
Lead Time: 21 days

⚠️ STOCKOUT RISK: Will run out before restock arrives

RECOMMENDATION: Order 350 units
- Covers 42 days of sales
- Storage cost: $47.25/month
- Stockout risk: 0%
- Overstock penalty: $0 (under 90-day threshold)

ALTERNATIVE: Order 500 units
- Covers 60 days of sales
- Storage cost: $67.50/month
- Overstock penalty: +$23.40 (aged inventory likely)
- ROI impact: -4.2%

DECISION: Order 350 (optimal balance)
```

### Portfolio Item for This

Create a Google Sheets template showing:
- Input section (velocity, lead time, storage tier, COGS)
- Cost comparison table (order 200 vs 300 vs 400 vs 500)
- Visual chart showing cost curves
- Clear recommendation output

**Title:** "FBA Restock Decision Calculator - Optimize Inventory ROI"

**Description:** "Automated tool that calculates optimal reorder quantities by balancing storage fees, stockout risk, and capital efficiency. Reduces over-ordering by 15-20% while maintaining 98%+ in-stock rate."

### Proposal Snippet for Restock Jobs

```
I saw you're looking to optimize your restock decisions across 50+ SKUs.

The challenge is balancing two risks: over-order (storage fees, tied-up capital)
vs under-order (stockouts, lost Buy Box). I build calculators that quantify
both sides and recommend optimal order quantities.

For one seller, this reduced aged inventory fees by 23% while keeping
stockout rate under 2%. Happy to show you how it works.

William
```
