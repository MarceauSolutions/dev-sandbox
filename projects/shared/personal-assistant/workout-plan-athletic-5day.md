# 5-Day Athletic Performance Split
**Focus:** Athleticism, Mobility, Functional Strength

---

## Weekly Structure
| Day | Focus | Primary Goal |
|-----|-------|--------------|
| Mon | **Legs (Power)** | Explosive lower body, hip mobility |
| Tue | **Push** | Horizontal/vertical pressing, shoulder mobility |
| Wed | **Pull** | Posterior chain, thoracic mobility |
| Thu | **Upper (Athletic)** | Full upper integration, rotational power |
| Fri | **Lower (Strength)** | Heavy compounds, ankle/hip stability |
| Sat-Sun | Active Recovery | Walk, stretch, play |

## Progressive Overload Guidelines

**Weeks 1-2:** Learn movements, establish baseline weights
**Weeks 3-4:** Add 5-10 lbs to main lifts OR 1-2 reps
**Week 5:** Deload (reduce volume 40%, keep intensity)
**Week 6+:** Start new cycle, increase baseline

## Key Principles

1. **Power Before Strength** — Always do explosive work fresh
2. **Mobility is Non-Negotiable** — Primer sets the session up for success
3. **Quality > Quantity** — Stop before form breaks
4. **Progressive Overload** — Track weights, beat the log
5. **Recovery Matters** — Sleep 7-8h, protein 0.8-1g/lb bodyweight

## Notes

- Warm-up: 5 min cardio (bike/row/jump rope) before mobility primer
- Rest days: Walk 20-30 min, light stretching, play a sport
- Swap exercises as needed (same movement pattern)
- Listen to your body — skip power work if CNS is fried
