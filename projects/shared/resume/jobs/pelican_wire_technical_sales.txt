Job Title: Technical Sales Representative
Company: Pelican Wire / Wire Experts Group
Department: Sales/Business Development
Reports To: Director of Sales
Employment Type: Full Time, Exempt
Location: Naples, FL

Description:
Pelican Wire, a leading custom wire and cable manufacturer, is seeking a technically savvy, growth-oriented Technical Sales Representative to drive profitable growth through new business development and strategic expansion within existing accounts. This role blends technical expertise, consultative selling, and business development to identify opportunities, solve customer challenges, and position Pelican as a trusted manufacturing and engineering partner. This is not a transactional sales role. You'll act as a technical problem solver and growth partner, working directly with customers to understand applications, uncover unmet needs, and deliver engineered wire and cable solutions that drive long-term value. You will shape solutions, influence design decisions, and build strategic partnerships that fuel sustainable, profitable growth. You'll also serve as the voice of the customer internally, translating market insights and technical challenges into opportunities for innovation, process improvement, and business expansion.

Key Duties & Responsibilities:

Business Development & Revenue Growth
- Identify, develop, and secure new business opportunities with prospective customers across target industries and strategic markets
- Expand revenue within existing accounts through consultative selling, strategic relationship development, and identification of new applications or programs
- Build and manage a strong, qualified pipeline through prospecting, networking, referrals, trade shows, and customer visits
- Develop account growth strategies that support long-term revenue, margin expansion, and market diversification
- Maintain regular customer contact through visits, calls, virtual meetings, and business reviews
- Travel regularly (25-50%) to support customer development, technical sales efforts, and strategic account management

Technical Sales & Customer Partnership
- Align Pelican's manufacturing capabilities with customer applications, specifications, and business objectives
- Lead technical discovery conversations by asking thoughtful questions that uncover customer requirements, risks, pain points, and future opportunities
- Analyze customer drawings, specifications, and application requirements to develop accurate, competitive, and technically sound solutions
- Prepare quotations, technical proposals, and specification packages for moderately complex projects
- Develop and deliver technical presentations, product demonstrations, and customer education sessions
- Build customer confidence by proactively addressing technical, commercial, and operational concerns throughout the sales cycle
- Support quote-to-order transfer to ensure technical accuracy, customer alignment, and successful operational execution
- Maintain knowledge of industry standards, certifications, competitive technologies, and emerging market trends

Strategic Collaboration & Voice of Customer
- Partner cross-functionally with engineering, operations, quality, supply chain, and marketing teams
- Serve as a technical liaison between customers, suppliers, and internal teams
- Capture and communicate voice-of-customer insights to support product enhancements and innovation
- Collaborate with internal teams to improve customer responsiveness, technical offerings, and market competitiveness
- Support process improvement, product development, and best practice initiatives
- Contribute to documentation, standardization, and knowledge-sharing efforts

Required Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Engineering, Technical Sales, Business, or related technical field required
- 3+ years of experience in technical sales, business development, applications engineering, or consultative sales within a manufacturing environment
- Wire & cable, plastics, industrial manufacturing, or engineered product experience preferred
- Demonstrated success developing new business and expanding strategic customer relationships
- Strong ability to influence, persuade, and negotiate in technical and commercial environments
- Proven ability to create trust and credibility with technical, purchasing, operational, and executive stakeholders
- Excellent communication and presentation skills with the ability to translate technical concepts for diverse audiences
- Strong analytical, project management, and problem-solving capabilities
- Ability to interpret technical drawings, specifications, and manufacturing capabilities
- Lean, Six Sigma, or continuous improvement experience preferred
- Self-starter with strong ownership mentality, strategic thinking, and ability to work autonomously

Key Success Metrics: New business revenue generation, existing account expansion, pipeline growth and conversion, gross margin performance, quote conversion rate, customer retention and strategic account growth, customer engagement effectiveness, cross-functional project execution
