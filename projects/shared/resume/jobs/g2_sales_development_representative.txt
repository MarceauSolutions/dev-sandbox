About The Role:
The Digital Markets Qualification Team qualifies software buyers for software vendor partners in the Pay-Per-Lead (PPL) program. This SDR role outreaches to software buyers, identifies if they have a need for software, and sets up appointments for the Sales teams.

In This Role, You Will:
- Actively connect with previously qualified and prospective software buyers via phone, SMS, email, and social media channels.
- Identify decision makers at businesses and match them up with monetizable PPL software markets.
- Pre-qualify buyers to determine their needs and match them with the best internal GDM associate to facilitate the needs conversation.
- Achieve quarterly quotas and daily KPIs of a minimum of 120 calls per day.
- Develop your sales skills through active participation in ongoing sales training.

Minimum Qualifications:
- 0-2 years of relevant sales experience (B2B or Software Sales experience a plus)
- Excellent verbal and written communication skills
- Proven track record of success in a goal-oriented environment
- Ability to build rapport and relationships with potential clients
- A desire for continuous learning and ability to give/receive feedback
- Readiness for a professional environment and a desire to grow a career in sales

What Can Help Your Application Stand Out:
- BA/BS degree preferred (Sales Programs/Concentrations a plus)

How We Use AI Technology In Our Hiring Process:
G2 incorporates AI-powered technology to assist with initial application screening, skills assessment, and identifying candidates whose qualifications align with role requirements. All final hiring decisions remain under human oversight.
