## 🚀 Campaign Execution

### Template to Use First
**Template 1: Case Study Lead** (highest converting, based on social proof)

```
Hi {owner_name}, this is William from Marceau Solutions.

I helped a Naples HVAC company capture $12K in new revenue their first month using Voice AI to answer calls 24/7.

Would a 5-min call to see if this could work for {business_name} be helpful?

Reply STOP to opt out.
```

### Follow-Up Sequence (Auto via SOP 19)
- Day 2: Still looking
- Day 5: Social proof
- Day 10: Direct question
- Day 15: Availability
- Day 30: Breakup
- Day 60: Re-engage

### Response Handling
- "YES" / "Interested" → Call within 1 hour or send Calendly
- "MORE INFO" → Send case study link + book call
- "HOW MUCH" → Send pricing + book call
- "NOT INTERESTED" → Thank them, mark CRM
- "STOP" → Auto opt-out (do not reply)

## ⚠️ Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. ❌ **Analysis paralysis** - Don't perfect the landing page for weeks. Ship it and iterate.
2. ❌ **Spray and pray** - Don't blast 1000 SMS. Start with 10, learn, optimize.
3. ❌ **Discounting too early** - Hold your price. If they say it's expensive, sell value.
4. ❌ **Not following up** - 80% of sales happen after the 5th touch. Use SOP 19.
5. ❌ **Over-delivering on first client** - Don't spend 100 hours on an $8K project. Productize.
