## Research Notes (Call Each One First)

### Research Template:
1. Call the number
2. Time how long to answer
3. Who answers? (Owner? Staff? Voicemail?)
4. How professional is the greeting?
5. Do they sound busy/stressed?
6. Can you hear background noise (kitchen, customers)?

**Why this matters:** If they answer in 2 rings with a professional greeting, they might not need Voice AI. If it goes to voicemail or takes 5+ rings, that's your pain point.
