### Agent 3: Customer Research (5/5)

**Target Persona**: "The Overwhelmed Independent"
- Owner-operator, 40+, 1-10 locations
- Working 60+ hours covering multiple roles
- 86% comfortable with AI adoption
- Daily revenue $1,200-$3,000, margins 3-6%

**Pain Level**: 9/10 (Critical/Existential)
- 70% have unfilled positions
- 80%+ annual turnover (130% for QSR)
- "2025 was their breaking point" - operators covering fry stations during holidays
- 150 missed calls/month = $27K lost annual revenue

**Current Spending**:
| Solution | Annual Cost |
|----------|-------------|
| Part-time phone staff | $26,000+ |
| AI voice solution | $2,400-6,000 |
| **Savings** | **77-91%** |

**Where They Congregate**:
- Reddit: r/restaurateur (9K+), r/KitchenConfidential (1M+)
- RestaurantOwner.com Forum (very active)
- National Restaurant Association (1M+ outlets)

**Trigger Events**:
1. Holiday staffing crisis (immediate)
2. Key employee quits (immediate)
3. Owner working entry-level positions (high urgency)
4. Calculating missed call revenue loss (high)

**Verdict**: Exceptional pain level with clear willingness to pay. Best timing ever - 2026 called "year of AI-driven restaurant".
