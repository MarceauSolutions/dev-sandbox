## Key Market Trends

### 1. Voice AI Becoming Mission-Critical (2026)

> "Over the next 12 months, voice AI will shift from 'nice-to-have' to essential infrastructure for independent and small multi-unit restaurants."
> — FSR Magazine, 2026 Tech Forecast

### 2. Independents Moving Faster Than Enterprise

Small operators (1-10 locations) are adopting voice AI faster than large chains because:
- No procurement bureaucracy
- Existential labor crisis
- Owner-operator decision making
- Lower accuracy requirements (phone vs drive-thru)

### 3. Multimodal AI Emerging

Shift from voice-only to:
- Video avatars
- Visual order confirmation
- Multilingual support
- Kiosk integration

### 4. Funding Surge

Voice AI startup funding surged 8x in 2024, indicating strong investor confidence in the category.
