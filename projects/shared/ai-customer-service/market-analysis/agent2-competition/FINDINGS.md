## Competitor Weaknesses to Exploit

### 1. Enterprise Focus Leaves SMB Gap

Most well-funded players target enterprise QSR chains:
- Long sales cycles (12-24 months)
- Complex integrations
- $500-5,000/month pricing
- Minimum location requirements

**Opportunity**: Target 1-10 location independents ignored by enterprise players

### 2. Drive-Thru Focus Ignores Phone Orders

Most competitors optimize for drive-thru:
- Requires 95%+ accuracy
- Hardware integration
- Complex menu handling

**Opportunity**: Phone order taking is simpler, lower accuracy threshold acceptable

### 3. No Video/Avatar Differentiation

All current solutions are voice-only:
- No visual confirmation
- No branded experience
- No multilingual lip-sync

**Opportunity**: Video avatar kiosks (Sora-enhanced) could be true differentiator

### 4. McDonald's Failure Creates Uncertainty

McDonald's ended IBM partnership due to accuracy issues:
- Shakes confidence in enterprise AI
- Creates pause in adoption
- Opens door for "proven" solutions

**Opportunity**: Position as battle-tested with smaller operators first

## Market Gaps Identified

### Gap 1: SMB-Focused Voice AI

**Current State**: Enterprise players ignore <10 location operators
**Opportunity**: Purpose-built for independents at $199-349/month
**Competition Level**: Low-Medium

### Gap 2: Phone Order Specialization

**Current State**: Focus on drive-thru, complex menu systems
**Opportunity**: Simple phone order taking for pizzerias, Chinese restaurants
**Competition Level**: Low

### Gap 3: Video Avatar Customer Service

**Current State**: All solutions are voice-only
**Opportunity**: Video avatar on kiosks, unique customer experience
**Competition Level**: Very Low (blue ocean)

### Gap 4: Vertical Integration with Other Services

**Current State**: Standalone voice AI products
**Opportunity**: Bundle with fitness influencer DM automation, Amazon seller support
**Competition Level**: None (unique to Marceau)

## Market Viability Score

**Score: 3/5 Stars**

**Rationale**:
- Intense competition at enterprise level (-2)
- $700M+ competitor funding is daunting (-1)
- BUT clear gaps in SMB segment (+1)
- Phone order niche underserved (+1)
- Video avatar is unexplored blue ocean (+1)

**Recommendation**: Avoid head-to-head with SoundHound/Presto. Target gaps.
