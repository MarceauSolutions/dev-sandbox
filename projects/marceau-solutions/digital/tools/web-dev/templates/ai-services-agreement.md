Specific scope details, integrations, and deliverables will be documented at kickoff and may be updated by mutual written agreement.

## 4. Cancellation

After the free trial period ends, either party may cancel this agreement with **30 days written notice** sent via email to the other party.

- Client's written notice should be sent to: wmarceau@marceausolutions.com
- Marceau Solutions' written notice will be sent to Client's email address on file.

Upon cancellation:
- Billing stops at the end of the current billing period (no partial refunds for unused days after the 30-day notice period has run).
- Client retains all data and call records that belong to them.
- Marceau Solutions will provide reasonable assistance to transition Client's phone number or data upon request.
