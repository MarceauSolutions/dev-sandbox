Common Questions

Straight answers. No runaround.

What's the catch with the free onboarding? +
There isn't one. I build your systems for free because I'm confident you'll see the value and want to continue. If you don't, you walk away with fully functional systems at no cost. I'd rather earn your business by proving value than by locking you into a contract.
What happens if I don't want the retainer after onboarding? +
You keep everything I built. The systems are yours. You can manage them yourself, and I'll make sure you're trained to do so. No hard feelings, no dismantling anything, no "gotcha" fees.
Are there any costs beyond the $295/mo retainer? +
Some AI tools have their own usage fees (for example, voice AI services charge per minute of phone time). I'll break down any third-party costs before we build anything, so there are never surprises. I don't mark up these costs — you pay exactly what the service charges.
How long does onboarding actually take? +
Two weeks from our first working session. Most systems are live and testable within the first week. The second week is for fine-tuning, testing with real scenarios, and making sure you're comfortable with everything.
What if I want to cancel the retainer later? +
Send me a message and it's done. No cancellation fees, no 30-day notice requirements, no exit interviews. Your systems keep running — you just manage them yourself going forward.
Do I need to be technical to use these systems? +
Not at all. Everything is designed to work without technical knowledge. During onboarding I'll train you on how to use and monitor your systems, and the retainer includes support for anything you can't figure out on your own.
I'm not sure if AI is right for my business. Should I still book a call? +
Absolutely. The discovery call is free and there's zero pressure. If AI isn't the right fit, I'll tell you directly and point you toward what might work better. I'd rather give you honest advice than sell you something you don't need.
