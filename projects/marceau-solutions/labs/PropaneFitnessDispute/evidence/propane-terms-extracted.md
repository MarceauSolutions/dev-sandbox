## Section 7: Refund Policy

### 7.1 Eligibility
"By accessing these materials, you therefore waive your right to the 14-day cooling-off period."

Full refunds available within 14 days if refund request form completed with evidence within 48 hours.

"After this initial period, refunds are not available for any reason."

**Note:** The claim that accessing materials waives the 14-day cooling-off period may be unenforceable under UK Consumer Contracts (Information, Cancellation and Additional Charges) Regulations 2013, which provides a statutory cooling-off period that cannot be waived by contract for digital services where specific informed consent was not obtained BEFORE payment.

### 7.2 Refund Requirements
Applicants must complete:
- Onboarding process
- Attend coaching calls
- Finish first 2 weeks' modules
- Engage twice with coach
- Raise concerns 72 hours prior to refund request
- Participate in 2 hotseat sessions
- Check in weekly
- Meet lead generation targets

### 7.3 Exclusions
Refunds denied for career changes, availability issues, schedule conflicts, or personal circumstances.

### 7.4 Process
Members submit refund request form to PropaneFitness.

**Key fact:** Cardholder formally requested this form on March 12. It was not provided until March 16 — 10 days later, attached to a legal threat.

### 7.5 Breach Consequences
"Any attempt to bypass our refund policy constitutes a breach of contract. This will result in immediate rejection of your refund request" plus potential counterclaims and collections referral.

**Key fact:** This clause is contradicted by Section 12.5 Exceptions, which preserves statutory rights including card issuer disputes. A clause purporting to treat the exercise of statutory rights as a breach of contract is an unfair term under Consumer Rights Act 2015, Part 2.

## Section 11: Liability

### 11.4
The company cannot limit liability for:
- Death or personal injury from negligence
- Fraud
- **Fundamental misrepresentation**
- Legally non-excludable obligations

**Key fact:** The nature of coaching (1-on-1 Zoom vs group clinics) is a fundamental matter. Propane's own terms acknowledge this liability cannot be excluded.

### 11.5
Company disclaims responsibility for indirect, consequential, or business interruption losses.

### 11.6
Liability capped at fees paid in the month before the liability arose.

## Section 12.5: Dispute Resolution

### Mandatory Process
1. Written notice (Days 1-7)
2. Good faith negotiation (Days 8-21)
3. CEDR mediation (Days 22-50)
4. Court action only after exhausting Steps 1-3

### Prohibited Without Process
"Initiating any of the following without first exhausting Steps 1-3 above constitutes a material breach."

### EXCEPTIONS (Critical)
**Urgent injunctive relief, debt collection post-default, and statutory rights remain available.**

**Key fact:** This Exceptions clause expressly preserves statutory rights — which include the right to file a chargeback dispute through a card issuer. This directly contradicts Propane's invocation of Section 7.5 to characterize chargebacks as a breach of contract.
