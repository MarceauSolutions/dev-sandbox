### IDEA #2 — SCADA & Remote Monitoring as a Service (RMaaS)

**Composite Score: 7.8/10**

#### Concept
Subscription-based remote monitoring service for small facilities that can't afford a full SCADA system. Deploy low-cost monitoring hardware (ESP32/Raspberry Pi + industrial sensors OR entry-level Ignition/AVEVA Edge), connect to cloud dashboard, charge monthly fee. Think of it as "SCADA-lite" for HOA pump stations, small manufacturers, marinas, and agricultural operations.

#### Target Customers
- HOA-managed pump stations and lift stations (Collier/Lee have hundreds)
- Small agricultural operations (eastern Collier, Hendry)
- Marina fuel dock operators
- Self-storage facility operators (HVAC monitoring)

#### Revenue Model
- Hardware install: $2,500-$8,000 per site (one-time)
- Monthly monitoring fee: $150-$500/site
- 20 sites at $250/month average = $5,000 MRR = $60,000/yr recurring

#### Why It Ranked #2 (Not #1)
- Higher setup complexity (hardware procurement, cloud infrastructure)
- Requires more capital upfront vs. pure service model
- Longer sales cycle
- **However:** This is the natural evolution of GCSP — every pump station you service becomes a monitoring candidate. RMaaS should be launched as GCSP Phase 2 (Year 2), not a standalone business.

#### 12-Month Projection (Conservative/Realistic/Optimistic)
- Conservative: 5 sites, $1,250/mo MRR by month 12, $8k total
- Realistic: 15 sites, $3,750/mo MRR by month 12, $25k total
- Optimistic: 30 sites, $7,500/mo MRR by month 12, $50k total

#### Startup Costs
- Hardware per site: $800-$2,500 (Ignition Edge license + hardware)
- Cloud server (EC2 or similar): $50-$150/mo
- Ignition Designer license: ~$3,000-$5,000 (or use Inductive Automation's cloud pricing)
- Total to launch first 3 sites: ~$7,000-$12,000

**Verdict:** Excellent as GCSP Year 2 add-on. Not the starting point.
