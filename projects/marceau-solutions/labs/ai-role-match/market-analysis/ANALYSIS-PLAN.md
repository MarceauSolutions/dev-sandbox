# Market Viability Analysis: AI Role-Match Hiring System

## Product Hypothesis
**What it is**: An AI-powered candidate ranking system that generates the "ideal job description" for each applicant, then uses mathematical similarity (k-means clustering, cosine similarity) to compare candidates' ideal roles against the actual open position -- matching jobs-to-jobs in the same vector space instead of keyword-matching resumes to jobs.
**Who it's for**: Mid-to-large companies (100-10,000 employees), recruiting agencies, and HR departments overwhelmed by applicant volume.
**Problem it solves**: Traditional ATS systems use keyword matching (resume-to-job), which is noisy and misses great candidates. This system compares in the same semantic space (job-to-job) for mathematically cleaner, more accurate matching.
**How we'd make money**: B2B SaaS subscription, per-seat or per-hire pricing.
