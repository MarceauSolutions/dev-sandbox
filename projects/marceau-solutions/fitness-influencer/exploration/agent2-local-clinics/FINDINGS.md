## CRITICAL: Florida Patient Brokering Act

**The Law (§ 817.505):**
- **FELONY** to offer/receive kickbacks for patient referrals
- Applies to ALL payers (not just Medicare)
- Covers direct payment, commissions, rebates, bonuses
- Up to 5 years prison + $5,000 fine

**SAFE Approaches:**
- Non-financial referral (you refer, they refer back, NO payment)
- Educational partnerships (workshops, content)
- Consulting arrangement (paid for expertise, NOT referrals)

**RISKY/ILLEGAL:**
- Direct referral fees
- Revenue share on treatments
- Commission per referred client

## Recommended Approach

1. **Start non-financial**: Build trust with educational collaboration
2. **Consult healthcare attorney** before any paid arrangement ($1,000-2,000)
3. **Target T Clinics USA first**: Newest, most entrepreneurial
4. **Position as**: "Fitness programming for peptide therapy clients"
