# Peptide Video Production Guide - Hybrid Approach

**Video**: "Peptides Explained: What Every Fitness Enthusiast Should Know in 2026"
**Approach**: Your Face (70%) + Sora 2 B-Roll (30%)
**Target Length**: 8-12 minutes

---

## Production Pipeline Overview

```
PHASE 1: PRE-PRODUCTION
├── Generate Nanobanana Pro starting frames (6 scenes)
├── Annotate frames with motion markers
├── Prep recording space for talking head
└── Review script, mark B-roll insertion points

PHASE 2: AI B-ROLL GENERATION
├── Feed annotated frames + prompts to Sora 2 Pro
├── Generate 6 B-roll clips (15-20 sec each)
├── Review and regenerate any failed clips
└── Export all B-roll at 4K

PHASE 3: TALKING HEAD RECORDING
├── Record all face-to-camera sections
├── Multiple takes for key moments
├── Capture room tone (10 sec silence)
└── Backup all footage

PHASE 4: POST-PRODUCTION
├── Assemble timeline with talking head as base
├── Insert B-roll at marked timestamps
├── Add text overlays (peptide names, categories)
├── Add graphics (frameworks, spectrum)
├── Color grade, audio mix, music
└── Export for YouTube + short-form clips
```

## TALKING HEAD RECORDING GUIDE

### Recording Environment Checklist

```
LIGHTING
□ Ring light positioned at eye level (key light)
□ Window light from side (fill light) OR second soft light
□ No harsh shadows on face
□ Background evenly lit (no hot spots)

AUDIO
□ Lavalier mic clipped on shirt (hidden if possible)
□ OR shotgun mic on boom just out of frame
□ Room echo minimized (soft furnishings help)
□ HVAC/fans OFF during recording
□ Phone on silent, notifications off
□ Record 10 seconds room tone before starting

CAMERA
□ Camera at eye level (not looking up or down)
□ Clean lens
□ 4K if available (1080p minimum)
□ Manual focus locked on face
□ f/2.8 or wider for background blur
□ Consistent color temperature setting

BACKGROUND
□ Clean, uncluttered
□ Relevant but not distracting (bookshelf, plant, etc.)
□ No windows directly behind (causes silhouette)
□ Depth - not flat against wall

YOU
□ Solid color shirt (no patterns, logos)
□ Hair/appearance camera-ready
□ Water nearby (stay hydrated)
□ Script/bullet points visible (phone or monitor)
□ Energized (coffee, stretch, whatever works)
```

### Section-by-Section Recording Notes

| Section | Duration | Energy Level | Key Delivery Notes |
|---------|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| **Hook** | 0:30 | HIGH | Lean in, direct eye contact, create urgency |
| **Section 1** | 2:00 | Medium-High | Educational but engaging, hand gestures OK |
| **Section 2** | 2:30 | Medium | Slower pace, let info land, use pauses |
| **Section 3** | 2:00 | Medium-Serious | "Real talk" tone, slight lean forward |
| **Section 4** | 2:00 | Authoritative/Warm | Expert advisor tone - "I've seen what works" |
| **Section 5** | 1:30 | Medium | Mentor tone, helpful and balanced |
| **CTA** | 0:30 | HIGH | Energy back up, clear ask, smile |

### Take Strategy

```
For each section:
1. Read through once (no recording) - get in flow
2. TAKE 1: Full section, don't stop for mistakes
3. TAKE 2: Full section again, usually better
4. TAKE 3: Only if major issues in Take 1-2
5. PICKUP TAKES: Re-record specific sentences that stumbled

Mark best takes in real-time: "That was good" after good takes
(helps in editing later)
```

## POST-PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

### Timeline Assembly Order

```
1. Import all talking head footage
2. Select best takes, arrange on timeline
3. Add jump cuts to remove:
   - Long pauses
   - "Um", "uh", filler words
   - False starts
   - Repeated sentences

4. Import Sora 2 B-roll clips
5. Insert B-roll at marked timestamps (see prompts above)
6. B-roll duration: 5-15 seconds max per insert
7. Overlap audio - your voice continues over B-roll

8. Add text overlays:
   - Peptide names when mentioned (lower third)
   - Category headers (full screen brief flash)
   - Key statistics

9. Add graphics:
   - Amino acid animation (Section 1)
   - "Proven to Hype" spectrum (Section 3)
   - Decision framework (Section 5)

10. Color grade:
    - Match B-roll to talking head color temp
    - Slight contrast boost
    - Consistent look throughout

11. Audio mix:
    - Voice: -6dB to -3dB (loudness normalized)
    - Music: -20dB to -15dB (under voice)
    - B-roll ambient: -18dB (subtle)

12. Add background music:
    - Lo-fi or subtle electronic
    - Lower during talking, slightly up during B-roll
    - Fade out before CTA

13. Add chapter markers:
    - 0:00 Hook
    - 0:30 What Are Peptides
    - 2:30 Categories
    - 5:00 Science vs Hype
    - 7:00 Personal Experience
    - 9:00 Decision Framework
    - 10:30 CTA

14. Export:
    - YouTube: 4K or 1080p, H.264, high bitrate
    - Thumbnail: 1920x1080 still frame
```

### Text Overlay Specs

```
PEPTIDE NAMES (Lower Third)
- Font: Inter Bold or SF Pro Bold
- Size: 48px
- Color: White with subtle drop shadow
- Position: Lower left, 10% from bottom
- Animation: Fade in 0.3s, hold, fade out 0.3s
- Duration: 3-4 seconds

CATEGORY HEADERS (Full Screen)
- Font: Inter Black
- Size: 120px
- Color: White
- Position: Center
- Animation: Scale in from 80%, hold, fade out
- Duration: 1.5 seconds
- Background: Semi-transparent dark overlay

STATISTICS
- Font: Inter Bold
- Size: 64px
- Color: Accent color (green for positive, amber for caution)
- Animation: Count up effect if number
```
