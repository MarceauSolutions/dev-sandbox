# Business Viability Testing Plan

**Purpose:** Validate client acquisition ability BEFORE taking on debt
**Timeline:** 2-4 weeks (pre-loan decision)
**Investment Required:** $0-500 (mostly time)

---

## Core Question

> "Can I consistently acquire paying clients for this business?"

If the answer is NO with $0-500 marketing spend, a loan won't fix it.

---

## Phase 1: Free Validation (Week 1-2)

### Test 1: Warm Network Outreach

**Objective:** Get 3-5 paying clients from personal network

**Actions:**
1. List everyone you know who:
   - Goes to the gym
   - Has expressed interest in fitness/health
   - Is 35+ and has disposable income
   - Lives in Naples or nearby

2. Reach out personally (call, text, in-person):
   ```
   "Hey [Name], I'm launching a personal training business
   focused on optimizing results for guys in their 30s-50s.
   I'm looking for a few founding clients to work with at a
   reduced rate while I build out my full program.
   Would you be interested in learning more?"
   ```

3. Offer founding client pricing:
   - Regular rate: $150/session
   - Founding rate: $75/session (50% off)
   - Commitment: 8 sessions minimum

**Success Metric:** 3+ people say yes and pay deposit

**If FAIL:** Business model may need adjustment (pricing, positioning, target market)

---

### Test 2: Gym Floor Conversations

**Objective:** Validate stranger acquisition without paid marketing

**Actions:**
1. Visit 3-5 gyms in Naples area (guest passes or day rates)
2. Work out during peak hours (5-7 PM weekdays, 9-11 AM weekends)
3. Start natural conversations with target demographic (35-55, affluent-looking)
4. Listen for pain points (plateau, injury recovery, time constraints)
5. Offer free consultation if genuine interest

**Target:** 10 conversations → 3 consultations → 1 client

**Script (natural conversation starter):**
```
"Hey, mind if I work in?" [after set]
"How long have you been training here?"
[Listen, build rapport]
"What are you working toward right now?"
[Listen for pain points]
[Only if relevant]: "That's actually what I specialize in.
I help guys break through plateaus using some newer protocols.
Happy to chat about it sometime if you're interested."
```

**Success Metric:** 2+ consultations booked from cold conversations

**If FAIL:** May need to adjust approach or target different venues

---

### Test 3: Social Media Organic Reach

**Objective:** Test content resonance with zero ad spend

**Actions:**
1. Post peptide video (when complete) to:
   - YouTube (long form)
   - Instagram Reel (60-sec version)
   - TikTok (60-sec version)

2. Post 5-7 additional pieces in 2 weeks:
   - Training tips (form corrections, programming)
   - Nutrition insights
   - Recovery protocols
   - Client transformation stories (with permission)

3. Include clear CTA:
   ```
   "DM me 'TRAIN' if you want to learn more about
   working together"
   ```

**Success Metrics:**
- 1,000+ views on at least one piece
- 10+ DMs received
- 2+ consultations booked

**If FAIL:** Content strategy needs adjustment, but doesn't invalidate business

## Phase 2: Paid Validation (Week 3-4)

**Only proceed if Phase 1 shows promise**

### Test 4: Instagram/Facebook Ads ($200-300)

**Objective:** Test paid acquisition at scale

**Setup:**
1. Create simple landing page (GitHub Pages - free):
   - Headline: "Naples Personal Training for Men 35+"
   - Subhead: "Science-based protocols for results that last"
   - Form: Name, Email, Phone, Goals
   - CTA: "Book Free Consultation"

2. Run ads targeting:
   - Location: Naples, FL (25-mile radius)
   - Age: 35-55
   - Gender: Male
   - Interests: Fitness, weight training, health optimization
   - Income: Top 25%

3. Ad creative:
   - Use best-performing organic content
   - Test 2-3 variations

**Budget:** $15-20/day for 14 days = $210-280

**Success Metrics:**
| Metric | Target | Red Flag |
|--------|--------|----------|
| CPM (cost per 1,000 views) | <$15 | >$30 |
| CTR (click-through rate) | >1% | <0.5% |
| CPC (cost per click) | <$2 | >$5 |
| Cost per lead | <$20 | >$50 |
| Lead → Consult rate | >30% | <15% |
| Consult → Client rate | >25% | <10% |

**Target:** $250 spend → 12+ leads → 4+ consultations → 1+ paying client

**If FAIL:** Either targeting is wrong or offer isn't compelling

---

### Test 5: Local Business Partnerships

**Objective:** Test B2B referral channel

**Actions:**
1. Identify complementary businesses:
   - Chiropractors (injury recovery angle)
   - Med spas (aesthetic/peptide overlap)
   - Country clubs (affluent demographic)
   - Golf pros (performance angle)

2. Propose referral arrangement:
   ```
   "I'm a personal trainer specializing in [their audience's needs].
   When clients ask about fitness, I'd love to be someone you
   can recommend. In return, I'll refer clients to you for
   [their service]. No money changes hands - just mutual referrals."
   ```

3. Offer to do a free workshop/talk for their clients

**Success Metric:** 2+ partnership agreements, 1+ referral received

**If FAIL:** May need different positioning or partners

## Key Metrics to Track

### Client Acquisition

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)** | Total marketing spend / New clients | <$100 |
| **Close Rate** | Consultations / Clients signed | >25% |
| **Lead → Consult Rate** | Leads / Consultations booked | >30% |
| **Time to First Client** | Days from first outreach to first payment | <14 days |

### Client Quality

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Session Frequency** | Sessions/week per client | 2-3 |
| **Retention (Month 1)** | Clients who continue past trial | >80% |
| **Referral Rate** | New clients from existing clients | 1 per 5 clients |

### Business Health

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| **Revenue per Client** | Monthly revenue / Active clients | >$400/month |
| **LTV (Lifetime Value)** | Avg months retained × Monthly revenue | >$2,000 |
| **LTV:CAC Ratio** | LTV / CAC | >10:1 |

## Go/No-Go Decision Framework

### GO Signals (Proceed with loan/full launch)

- [ ] 5+ paying clients acquired through testing
- [ ] At least 2 acquisition channels working
- [ ] CAC under $100
- [ ] Close rate above 25%
- [ ] Clients expressing satisfaction / willing to refer
- [ ] Clear path to 10+ clients within 60 days

### PAUSE Signals (Adjust before proceeding)

- [ ] 3-4 paying clients but unclear which channel works
- [ ] CAC between $100-200
- [ ] Close rate 15-25%
- [ ] Mixed client feedback

**Action:** Refine positioning, pricing, or targeting. Retest for 2 weeks.

### NO-GO Signals (Do not take loan)

- [ ] <3 paying clients after 4 weeks of testing
- [ ] CAC over $200
- [ ] Close rate under 15%
- [ ] Can't get consultations booked
- [ ] Nobody in network interested

**Action:** Business model needs fundamental rethinking. Consider:
- Different niche
- Different location
- Online-only model
- Part-time while employed
