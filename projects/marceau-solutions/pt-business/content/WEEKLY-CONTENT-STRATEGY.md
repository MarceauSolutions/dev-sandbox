## The Hormozi Framework

Based on Alex Hormozi's $4M content strategy ([source](https://www.linkedin.com/posts/alexhormozi_my-4000000-content-strategy-for-2025-activity-7251286824633970688-maaR)):

### Core Principles
1. **One idea, many outputs** - Create one piece of long-form content, repurpose into 10+ pieces
2. **250+ posts/week** - High volume, but quality > quantity
3. **Three pillars**: Education, Authenticity, Actionable Advice
4. **Platform-specific adaptation** - Same message, different format per platform

### Weekly Pattern (Hormozi Style)
| Day | Content Theme |
|-----|---------------|
| Monday | Motivational / Mindset |
| Tuesday | Tactical Tips / How-To |
| Wednesday | Tactical Business/Fitness Advice |
| Thursday | Q&A / Engagement |
| Friday | Case Studies / Transformations |
| Saturday | Engagement / Community |
| Sunday | Lifestyle / Behind-the-scenes |

## Video Format Guide

Based on [Riverside](https://riverside.fm/blog/hormozi-style-videos) and [OpusClip](https://www.opus.pro/blog/create-videos-in-alex-hormozi-style-the-ultimate-guide):

### Talking Head (Primary - 60% of content)
- Direct eye contact with camera
- Bold, animated captions
- Simple background
- Personal connection with audience
- Best for: Tips, advice, motivational content

### B-Roll Overlay (30% of content)
- Talking head in corner (picture-in-picture)
- B-roll footage of workouts, lifestyle
- Text overlays highlighting key points
- Best for: Workout demonstrations, day-in-the-life

### Pure B-Roll (10% of content)
- Voiceover only
- High-energy workout footage
- Music with captions
- Best for: Transformation reveals, workout montages

### Hormozi Editing Style
- Quick cuts (every 2-3 seconds)
- Bold colored captions (yellow/green/white)
- Sound effects on transitions
- Zoom in on key points
- Light flare transitions
- Emojis in captions

## Content Mix Strategy

Based on [edutainment research](https://www.teamlewis.com/magazine/mastering-edutainment-how-to-create-content-thats-fun-and-informative/):

### The 80/20 Rule
- **80%**: Educate, Entertain, or Inspire
- **20%**: Promote/Sell

### Content Categories

| Category | % of Content | Examples |
|----------|--------------|----------|
| **Educational** | 40% | Workout tips, form corrections, nutrition advice |
| **Entertainment** | 25% | Challenges, trends, personality-driven content |
| **Inspirational** | 15% | Transformations, mindset, motivation |
| **Behind-the-Scenes** | 10% | Day in life, prep, personal stories |
| **Promotional** | 10% | Services, programs, calls-to-action |

### Why Edutainment Wins
- Higher retention than pure education
- More shareable than pure entertainment
- Builds authority + likability simultaneously
- Algorithm rewards engagement (edutainment gets both)

## Platform-Specific Adaptations

### X/Twitter Strategy
Based on [Brand24](https://brand24.com/blog/twitter-tips/) and [Exercise.com](https://www.exercise.com/grow/how-to-start-a-fitness-twitter-account/):

- **Format**: Text + image/video
- **Length**: 280 chars max, punchy and quotable
- **Hashtags**: 2-3 relevant only
- **Frequency**: 3-5 posts/day
- **Best content**: Quick tips, hot takes, threads, workout clips
- **Engagement**: Reply to comments within 1 hour

### Instagram Strategy
- **Feed**: Polished, educational, carousel posts
- **Reels**: Entertainment-forward, trending audio
- **Stories**: Daily, casual, polls/questions
- **Frequency**: 1-2 feed, 1-2 reels, 5-10 stories daily

### TikTok Strategy
- **Format**: Vertical video, native trends
- **Style**: Less polished, more authentic
- **Hooks**: First 1-2 seconds critical
- **Frequency**: 2-4 posts/day ideal
- **Best content**: Trends, challenges, quick tips

## Content Production Workflow

### Weekly Planning (Friday)
1. Review analytics from past week
2. Identify top performers to replicate
3. Plan 1-2 long-form pieces (YouTube/podcast)
4. Break down into 20+ short-form pieces
5. Schedule in content queue

### Daily Execution
1. **Morning (6-7 AM)**: Post scheduled content, check engagement
2. **Midday (12-1 PM)**: Respond to comments, post second wave
3. **Evening (7-9 PM)**: Post entertainment content, engage
4. **Night (before bed)**: Schedule next day's early posts

### Repurposing Chain
```
Long-form video (YouTube)
    ├── 5-10 short clips (Reels/TikTok/Shorts)
    ├── Audio → Podcast episode
    ├── Transcript → 3-5 X threads
    ├── Key points → Carousel posts
    └── Quotes → Text posts with images
```
