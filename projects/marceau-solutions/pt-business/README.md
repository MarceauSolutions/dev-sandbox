# William Marceau - Personal Training Business

Online personal training coaching. Peptide-focused, evidence-based training. $197/month.

## Architecture

```
fitness-influencer/  = The PRODUCT (SaaS tool for fitness influencers)
pt-business/         = William's BUSINESS (first customer of the product)
```

This directory contains all **business operations**: client documents, content strategy, financial planning, market research, and operational data. The product code, backend APIs, and technical infrastructure live in `fitness-influencer/`.

## What's Here

| Directory | Contents |
|-----------|----------|
| `ops/` | Coaching operations runbook (the master doc) |
| `legal/` | Cancellation policy, liability waiver, working-together agreement (MD + PDF) |
| `intake/` | 35-question intake form for Google Forms |
| `content/` | Content strategy, weekly calendars, peptide video production assets |
| `business-planning/` | 36-month financial projections, viability testing, funding prep |
| `research/` | Peptide business viability study (4-agent analysis) |
| `data/` | Midweek coaching tips, collaborator tracking |
| `workflows/` | Business workflow SOPs (future) |
