## Production

- [ ] Response dashboard with hot lead highlighting

**Scene 3: Social Proof (1:45-2:00)**
- [ ] Metrics slide (70% time reduction, 3x response rates)

**Scene 4: CTA (2:00-2:15)**
- [ ] Calendar booking page or simple contact method
- [ ] Friendly, low-pressure closing

### Tools
