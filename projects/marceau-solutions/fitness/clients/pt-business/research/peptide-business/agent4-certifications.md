## Critical Finding: Legal Protection

**Certifications DO NOT:**
- Shield you from prosecution
- Make illegal activities legal
- Protect against client lawsuits
- Guarantee insurance coverage

**Certifications DO:**
- Show good faith effort to educate yourself
- Provide credibility with clients
- May help in legal proceedings as mitigation
- Separate you from untrained competitors

## The RUO Model (How Legal Coaches Operate)

1. Position as EDUCATOR about peptide science
2. Clients source their own peptides (through their doctor)
3. You coach on implementation, NOT prescribe
4. Clear disclaimers everywhere
5. No product sales, no dosing guidance

**Warning:** Even RUO model is risky - FDA is cracking down on businesses that appear to market for human use.

## Competitor Positioning Pattern

Top peptide coaches:
1. Foundational fitness credential (CPT, ISSN)
2. Longevity specialty (SSRP or A4M)
3. Personal brand authority (content, results)
4. Position as "educator" not "prescriber"
5. Refer to licensed providers for actual prescriptions
