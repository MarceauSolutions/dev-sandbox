# Client Intake Form - Questions for Google Forms

**Instructions**: Create a new Google Form, copy these questions in order. Set required fields as noted.

---

## Section 1: Basic Information

1. **Full Name** (Short answer, REQUIRED)

2. **Email Address** (Short answer, REQUIRED)

3. **Phone Number** (Short answer, REQUIRED)
   - Description: "For weekly check-in texts. US format preferred."

4. **Age** (Short answer, REQUIRED)

5. **Time Zone** (Dropdown, REQUIRED)
   - Description: "So I can schedule check-ins at a reasonable time for you."
   - Options:
     - Eastern (ET)
     - Central (CT)
     - Mountain (MT)
     - Pacific (PT)
     - Alaska (AKT)
     - Hawaii (HT)
     - Other (I'll specify below)

---

## Section 2: Health & Medical History

6. **Do you have any current injuries or physical limitations?** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
   - Description: "Be specific. Bad knee, shoulder impingement, herniated disc, etc. If none, type 'None'."

7. **Do you have any diagnosed medical conditions?** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
   - Description: "Diabetes, high blood pressure, thyroid issues, etc. If none, type 'None'."

8. **Are you currently taking any medications or supplements?** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
   - Description: "Include dosages if known. If none, type 'None'."

9. **Have you been cleared by a physician to exercise?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
   - Yes
   - No, but I plan to get clearance
   - I haven't asked, but I have no known issues

10. **Any history of eating disorders or disordered eating?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - No
    - Yes, but it's resolved
    - Yes, currently managing
    - Prefer not to say

---

## Section 3: Training Background

11. **How long have you been training consistently?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - Complete beginner (0-3 months)
    - Novice (3-12 months)
    - Intermediate (1-3 years)
    - Advanced (3+ years)

12. **What does your current training look like?** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
    - Description: "How many days/week, what type of training, how long per session. If not currently training, describe your most recent routine."

13. **What equipment do you have access to?** (Checkboxes, REQUIRED)
    - Full commercial gym
    - Home gym with barbell + rack
    - Dumbbells only
    - Resistance bands
    - Bodyweight only
    - Cable machine
    - Cardio equipment (treadmill, bike, etc.)
    - Other (specify)

14. **How many days per week can you realistically train?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - 2 days
    - 3 days
    - 4 days
    - 5 days
    - 6 days

15. **How much time per session?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - 30 minutes
    - 45 minutes
    - 60 minutes
    - 75+ minutes

16. **Any exercises you can't do or strongly dislike?** (Paragraph)
    - Description: "E.g., 'No overhead pressing due to shoulder', 'I hate running'."

---

## Section 4: Goals

17. **What is your PRIMARY goal?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - Lose body fat
    - Build muscle
    - Improve strength
    - Body recomposition (lose fat + build muscle)
    - Improve overall fitness and health
    - Athletic performance
    - Optimize hormones / longevity

18. **Be specific about your goal.** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
    - Description: "E.g., 'Lose 20lbs', 'Bench 225', 'Get visible abs', 'Run a 5K'. The more specific, the better."

19. **What have you tried before that didn't work?** (Paragraph)
    - Description: "Diets, programs, coaches, apps. What failed and why do you think it didn't stick?"

20. **On a scale of 1-10, how committed are you to following a program consistently for 3+ months?** (Linear scale, REQUIRED)
    - Scale: 1 (Not very) to 10 (All in)

---

## Section 5: Nutrition

21. **How would you describe your current diet?** (Paragraph, REQUIRED)
    - Description: "No judgment. Just describe what a typical day of eating looks like."

22. **Any dietary restrictions or allergies?** (Paragraph)
    - Description: "Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, lactose intolerant, etc. If none, type 'None'."

23. **Have you tracked macros before?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - Yes, I track regularly
    - I've tried but didn't stick with it
    - No, but I'm willing to learn
    - No, and I'm not interested in tracking

24. **How many meals per day do you typically eat?** (Multiple choice, REQUIRED)
    - 2 meals
    - 3 meals
    - 4+ meals
    - Intermittent fasting

## Section 6: Lifestyle & Recovery

    - Sedentary (desk job, minimal movement)
    - Lightly active (some walking, occasional standing)

- **Limit to 1 response**: No (allow edits)

- **Response destination**: Google Sheets (new spreadsheet: "Coaching Intake Responses")
