# Cancellation & Refund Policy

**William Marceau Fitness Coaching**

---

## Subscription Terms

- **Monthly subscription:** $197/month
- **Billing cycle:** Recurring on the same date each month
- **Payment processor:** Stripe

---

## Cancellation

- You may cancel your subscription **at any time** with no cancellation fees.
- Cancel through your Stripe billing portal (link provided in your welcome email) or by contacting wmarceau@marceausolutions.com.
- Cancellation takes effect at the **end of your current billing period**. You retain access to coaching and communication until that date.
- Your training program PDFs and Google Drive folder remain accessible after cancellation.

---

## Refunds

- **No refunds** for partial months. If you cancel mid-cycle, you retain access through the end of your paid period.
- If you experience a billing error or unauthorized charge, contact wmarceau@marceausolutions.com and it will be resolved within 48 hours.

---

## Pausing

- If you need to take a break (travel, injury, life events), contact me and we can **pause your subscription** for up to 30 days at no charge.
- Pauses are handled manually. Just send a text or email.

---

## Returning Clients

- Former clients are always welcome back.
- No re-enrollment fees. Just resubscribe and we pick up where we left off.

---

## Failed Payments

- If your payment fails, Stripe will automatically retry over the next 7 days.
- I'll reach out via text to let you know.
- If payment cannot be resolved within 14 days, your subscription will be cancelled and you'll move to the offboarding process.
- No penalty. No hard feelings. We'll sort it out.
