## Weekly Cadence

**Monday morning** - You'll get a check-in text. Reply with:
- A rating from 1-10 (how your body feels)
- Any notes (soreness, energy, sleep, stress, wins)

**Sunday evening** - Your updated program for the next week goes into your Google Drive folder. I'll send you an email heads-up.

**Monthly** - We do a 15-minute Google Meet progress review. I'll send you a Calendly link to book it.

## What I Need From You

- **Monday check-in response** - Even just a number is fine. This is how I know what to adjust.
- **Honest progress photos monthly** - Front, side, back. Same lighting, same time of day. These stay in YOUR Google Drive folder.
- **48 hours notice for reschedules** - Life happens, but please give me a heads-up.
- **Tell me when something isn't working** - A program only works if you can actually do it. I'd rather adjust than have you struggle in silence.

## How to Get the Most Out of This

1. **Follow the program as written** for at least 2 weeks before requesting changes
2. **Track your workouts** - reps, sets, weights. This data drives better programming.
3. **Be honest about compliance** - If you skipped 3 workouts, tell me. I can't help what I can't see.
4. **Ask questions** - There's no such thing as a dumb question about your own body.
5. **Trust the process** - Real results take 8-12 weeks minimum. We're playing the long game.
