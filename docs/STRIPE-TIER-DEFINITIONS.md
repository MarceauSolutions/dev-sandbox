## Automation Setup Tiers (One-Time Project Fees)

### Tier 1: Starter - $2,997

**Description:** "Single workflow, 2-3 integrations"

**What's Included:**
- **1 automated workflow** (e.g., contact form → CRM → auto-response)
- **2-3 integrations:**
  - Example: Website + ClickUp CRM + Twilio SMS
  - Example: Website + Google Sheets + Email notifications
- **Basic AI processing** (if applicable)
- **Training:** 2-3 hours

**Deliverables:**
- Contact form on website
- Leads captured in CRM (ClickUp or Google Sheets)
- Auto-response SMS or email
- Documentation for client to manage

**Best For:**
- Small businesses just starting with automation
- Single pain point to solve (e.g., "stop missing leads from website")
- Limited budget ($3K range)
- 1-5 employees

**Examples:**
- Restaurant: Online order form → ClickUp → SMS confirmation
- Plumber: Contact form → Google Sheets → Email auto-response
- Gym: Membership inquiry form → ClickUp → SMS follow-up

**Time to Deliver:** 1-2 weeks

**Best For:**

## Retainer Tiers (Monthly Ongoing Support)

### Maintenance Retainer - $747/month

**Stripe Description:** "Ongoing support and maintenance for your AI automation systems. Includes bug fixes, minor tweaks, platform updates, and up to 5 hours of support per month. Quarterly check-in calls to ensure everything runs smoothly."

**What's Included:**
- ✅ Bug fixes and error resolution
- ✅ Platform/API updates as services change
- ✅ Up to **5 hours of support monthly**
- ✅ Quarterly optimization check-in
- ✅ Email support (48-hour response)

**What's NOT Included:**
- ❌ Proactive optimization
- ❌ New feature builds
- ❌ Strategy calls
- ❌ Priority/Slack support

**Best For:**
- Starter and Growth setup clients
- Businesses that need automations maintained without active optimization
- Stable operations (not rapidly changing)
- Clients who can manage day-to-day operations themselves

**Example Monthly Activities:**
- Fix broken integration (API changed, automation stopped working)
- Update Voice AI knowledge base (new service added)
- Adjust call routing (new employee hired)
- Platform maintenance (Twilio version update, ClickUp API changes)

---

### Partner Retainer - $2,247/month

**Stripe Description:** "Strategic partnership for continuous AI optimization and growth. Includes 10 hours of optimization work monthly, priority support, monthly strategy calls, and proactive system improvements. Your dedicated AI automation partner."

**What's Included:**
- ✅ **10 hours of optimization/development monthly**
- ✅ Monthly strategy calls (30-60 min)
- ✅ Priority support (24-hour response)
- ✅ Proactive monitoring and improvements
- ✅ New feature builds as needed
- ✅ Slack/direct communication channel
- ✅ Strategic AI guidance

**What's NOT Included:**
- ❌ Major new projects (new integrations beyond 10 hrs = separate project fee)
- ❌ Content creation (blog posts, social media)
- ❌ Paid advertising management

**Best For:**
- Enterprise clients
- Fast-growing businesses (scaling operations)
- Clients who want continuous optimization (not just maintenance)
- Businesses that need strategic AI guidance
- High-value clients who need proactive improvements

**Example Monthly Activities:**
- Monthly strategy call: Review analytics, identify growth opportunities
- Optimize Voice AI: A/B test greeting scripts to improve booking rate
- Build new feature: Add appointment reminder automation
- CRM optimization: Create custom dashboard for owner
- Proactive improvement: Notice call volume spike, adjust routing before client asks
- Slack support: Answer questions within 24 hours
- New automation: Build SMS follow-up sequence for lost opportunities

## Tier Selection Guide (For Sales)

### How to Recommend the Right Tier:

**Ask these questions during discovery:**

1. **How many locations do you have?**
   - 1 location → Starter or Growth
   - 2-5 locations → Enterprise

2. **How many employees?**
   - 1-5 → Starter
   - 5-20 → Growth
   - 20+ → Enterprise

3. **What's your main pain point?**
   - Single issue (missing calls) → Starter
   - Multiple issues (calls + follow-up + booking) → Growth
   - Complex operations (multi-location + inventory + scheduling) → Enterprise

4. **What's your monthly revenue?**
   - <$50K/mo → Starter
   - $50K-300K/mo → Growth
   - >$300K/mo → Enterprise

5. **Do you have internal tech/marketing staff?**
   - No → Recommend Partner Retainer
   - Yes, but limited → Recommend Maintenance Retainer
   - Yes, capable → Recommend no retainer (hand-off)

6. **How many integrations do you need?**
   - 2-3 (Website, CRM, Email) → Starter
   - 4-6 (Website, Voice AI, CRM, Calendar, SMS, Email) → Growth
   - 6+ (Add payment, inventory, analytics) → Enterprise

## Time Investment Per Tier

### Setup Time:
- Starter: 15-20 hours
- Growth: 30-40 hours
- Enterprise: 60-80 hours

### Monthly Retainer Time:
- Maintenance: 5-8 hours/month per client
- Partner: 15-20 hours/month per client

### Capacity Analysis:

**If you work 40 hrs/week = 160 hrs/month:**

**Option A: Focus on Setup (No Retainers)**
- Deliver 4 Growth clients/month (40 hrs each) = 160 hrs
- Revenue: 4 × $7,497 = $29,988/month = **$359,856/year**

**Option B: Balanced (Setup + Retainers)**
- Deliver 2 Growth clients/month (80 hrs)
- Support 5 Partner retainers (80 hrs)
- Revenue: (2 × $7,497) + (5 × $2,247) = $14,994 + $11,235 = **$26,229/month** = $314,748/year

**Option C: Retainer-Heavy (Stable Recurring)**
- Deliver 1 Enterprise client/month (60-80 hrs)
- Support 4 Partner retainers (60-80 hrs)
- Revenue: $14,997 + (4 × $2,247) = **$23,985/month** = $287,820/year

**Takeaway:** Option A (focus on setup, minimal retainers) = highest revenue but least stable.
