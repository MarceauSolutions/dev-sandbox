**Goal:** $10K-15K/month from 3-4 HVAC clients

**Offer:** "Never Miss Another Emergency Call" - Complete automation package
**Includes:**
- Website (if needed or existing site is terrible)
- Voice AI (24/7 call answering, appointment booking)
- CRM integration (customer tracking, follow-up automation)
- Emergency dispatch workflow
- Maintenance reminder system

**Pricing:** $15K implementation + $3K-5K/month retainer

**Target Customer:**
- HVAC companies in Naples/SW Florida
- 5-20 employees
- $500K-$3M annual revenue
- Missing 20-40% of calls (losing $50K-100K/year)
- Low tech adoption (paper invoices, Excel, basic website or none)

### Month-by-Month Execution

**Month 1 (Jan 19 - Feb 15):**
- Week 1-2: Install Voice AI on Sr.'s business, track results
- Week 3-4: Use case study to target first paying client
- **Target:** 1 signed contract ($15K + $3K/month)

**Month 2 (Feb 16 - Mar 15):**
- Use Client #1 results as case study #2
- Expand territory (Fort Myers, Bonita Springs)
- Target: 2 more clients
- **Revenue:** $45K one-time + $9K/month recurring

**Month 3 (Mar 16 - Apr 15):**
- Add 4th client OR launch productized offer (Voice AI only at $5K + $750/mo)
- **Revenue:** $10K-15K/month recurring

**Months 4-6 (Apr 16 - Jul 15):**
- Stabilize existing clients (ensure 95%+ satisfaction)
- Document common workflows and pain points
- Prepare for Phase 2 productization
- **Revenue:** $12K-18K/month recurring

### Success Criteria
- ✅ 3-4 full retainer clients ($3K-5K/month each)
- ✅ $10K-15K monthly recurring revenue
- ✅ 95%+ client satisfaction (NPS score)

## Phase 2: Productize Common Workflows (Months 7-12)
**Goal:** $5K-10K/month from SaaS products + maintain agency revenue

### What We're Building
**Based on client patterns (hypothetical examples):**

**Product 1: HVAC Call Commander** (if all clients need emergency dispatch)
- Voice AI pre-configured for HVAC terminology
- Integrates with common HVAC scheduling tools (ServiceTitan, Housecall Pro)
- Emergency vs routine call routing
- **Price:** $299/month
- **Target:** Smaller HVAC companies (1-5 employees) who can't afford $3K retainer

**Product 2: HVAC Maintenance Automator** (if all clients need maintenance reminders)
- Automated seasonal maintenance reminders to customers
- SMS/email campaigns ("Time for your AC tune-up!")
- Upsell opportunities (filter replacements, duct cleaning)
- **Price:** $199/month
- **Target:** HVAC companies with 500+ residential customers

**Product 3: Elderly HVAC Care AI** (if clients ask for elder-specific workflows)
- Your "fun idea" now validated by client demand!
- Special protocols for elderly customers (slower speech, clearer explanations, caregiver notifications)
- Emergency escalation for vulnerable customers
- **Price:** $399/month (premium pricing for specialized feature)
- **Target:** HVAC companies in retirement communities (Naples, FL is perfect market)

### Productization Strategy

**Step 1: Extract from Client Work (Months 7-8)**
- Review all 3-4 client implementations
- Identify exact workflows ALL clients paid for
- Extract common code into standalone product
- Create product-specific landing page

**Step 2: Offer to Existing Clients First (Month 9)**
- Present as "upgrade" or "add-on module"
- Pricing: $199-399/month (cheaper than custom retainer work)
- Test product with friendly clients before public launch
- Refine based on feedback

**Step 3: Public Launch (Months 10-12)**
- Launch to HVAC companies NOT on retainer
- Use existing client results as case studies
- Target: 20-30 SaaS customers @ $199-399/month = $4K-12K/month
- **Total Revenue:** $12K-18K (agency) + $4K-12K (SaaS) = $16K-30K/month

### Success Criteria
- ✅ 1-2 SaaS products launched
- ✅ 20-30 SaaS customers
- ✅ $5K-10K/month SaaS revenue (additional to agency)
- ✅ 80%+ retention rate on SaaS products
- ✅ Products validated by REAL client demand (not speculation)

- No nesting (all siblings at same level)
- Easy to archive client work when done
