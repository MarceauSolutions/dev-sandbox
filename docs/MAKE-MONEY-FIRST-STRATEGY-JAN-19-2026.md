## What the Research Revealed

### AI Products (SaaS) = Slow Money
- Need 100-200 customers to hit $10K/month
- 77% annual churn for products <$50/month
- 6-12 months to $10K MRR (if successful)
- Market saturated: 16,955+ MCPs already exist
- **Verdict:** Wrong first move - too slow, too risky

### Agency Services = Fast Money
- Need 2-4 clients to hit $10K/month
- 3-6 months to $10K MRR (more predictable)
- Lower churn (12-24 month retention)
- Niche specialists charge 40-60% premium
- **Verdict:** RIGHT first move - fast, predictable, validates ideas

## The 90-Day Plan: HVAC Agency Only

### Why HVAC?
1. **Family connection** - Sr.'s business is free pilot/case study
2. **Proven demand** - HVAC companies lose $10K-50K/year from missed calls
3. **Clear ROI** - Easy to measure (calls answered, appointments booked, revenue recovered)
4. **Low competition** - Saturation: 5/10 (vs restaurants at 8/10)
5. **High pricing** - $15K setup + $3K-5K/month retainer

### What We're Selling
**Product:** "Never Miss Another Emergency Call"
**Offer:** Voice AI + CRM + Website (if needed) + Appointment Booking
**Pricing:** $15K implementation + $3K/month retainer
**ROI Promise:** Recover $50K-100K in lost revenue annually

### Target Customer
- **Company size:** 5-20 employees, $500K-$3M annual revenue
- **Location:** Naples, Fort Myers, Bonita Springs (local only - face-to-face advantage)
- **Pain point:** Missing 20-40% of calls when on job sites
- **Tech level:** Low (still using paper invoices, Excel spreadsheets)
