## Production Notes (Humiston Formula)

Every long-form video follows this structure:

1. **Hook** (0-15 sec): Bold claim or surprising statement. "Most people with [condition] are training completely wrong. Here's what the science actually says."
2. **Problem** (15 sec - 2 min): Clearly articulate the pain point. Make the viewer feel seen.
3. **Science** (2-5 min): Evidence-based explanation. Reference studies. Use visuals/graphics. Be the smart friend.
4. **Solution** (5-8 min): Specific, actionable protocol. Show exactly what to do.
5. **Demo** (8-10 min): Film yourself doing it. Real gym. Real movements. Show modifications for limitations.
6. **CTA** (last 30 sec): "If you want a complete system built for YOUR body, link in bio for a free strategy call."

**Shorts/Reels formula**: Extract the single best 30-60 second clip from the long-form. Add text overlay. Hook in first 2 seconds.
