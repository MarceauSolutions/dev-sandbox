# Business Model Options Analysis - Marceau Solutions

**Date:** 2026-01-19
**Context:** You've built websites + Voice AI + CRM for 2 clients. Now questioning if "managing their entire business operations" is the right model.

---

## What You've Proven You CAN Do

✅ Build professional websites with contact forms
✅ Set up Voice AI phone systems (Twilio + Claude)
✅ Configure CRM systems (ClickUp)
✅ Create marketing automation (auto-responses, routing)
✅ Build multi-tenant systems (one codebase, multiple businesses)

**The question:** Should you manage all of this for each client ongoing, or just build it and hand it off?

---

## Business Model Option 1: "Done-For-You + Managed Service" (Current Model)

**What you sell:**
- Build website + Voice AI + CRM ($8K setup)
- Manage it ongoing ($200-500/mo per client)
- You're their outsourced tech/marketing team

**Revenue Model:**
- 10 clients × $8K = $80K (one-time)
- 10 clients × $400/mo × 12 = $48K/year recurring
- **Year 1: $128K** | **Year 2: $48K**

**Pros:**
- ✅ Recurring revenue (predictable income)
- ✅ Deep client relationships (they depend on you)
- ✅ Upsell opportunities (add-ons, expansions)

**Cons:**
- ❌ You become "tech support" for 10 businesses
- ❌ Client churn kills revenue (lose 1 client = -$400/mo)
- ❌ Hard to scale (time-intensive per client)
- ❌ You're managing their leads, their CRM, their customers (high responsibility)

**Time Required:**
- Setup: 20-30 hrs per client
- Monthly: 3-5 hrs per client
- **10 clients = 30-50 hrs/month ongoing**

**Scalability:** LOW (capped at ~10-15 clients before you need to hire)

---

## Business Model Option 2: "Build & Handoff" (One-Time Projects)

**What you sell:**
- Build website + Voice AI + CRM ($8K-15K one-time)
- Train them to use it
- Hand it off (they manage it themselves)
- Optional: $500 support retainer if they want help

**Revenue Model:**
- 20 clients/year × $10K = $200K (one-time)
- 5 clients × $500/mo support = $30K/year (optional)
- **Year 1: $230K** | **Year 2: $230K** (new clients)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Higher per-project revenue (charge more for handoff)
- ✅ No ongoing management burden
- ✅ Scalable (can do 2-3 projects/month)
- ✅ No client churn risk

**Cons:**
- ❌ No recurring revenue (feast/famine)
- ❌ Need constant new client acquisition
- ❌ Less predictable income

**Time Required:**
- Setup: 20-30 hrs per client
- Training: 5-8 hrs
- Ongoing: 0 hrs (unless they buy support)
- **20 clients/year = 500-760 hrs/year = 10-15 hrs/week**

**Scalability:** MEDIUM (can scale to 2-3 projects/month = $20K-30K/mo)

---

## Business Model Option 3: "SaaS Platform" (Software Product)

**What you sell:**
- Multi-tenant platform (like you built for HVAC + Shipping)
- Businesses sign up, get website + Voice AI + CRM
- Self-service setup (templates, wizards)
- Subscription model ($200-500/mo per business)

**Revenue Model:**
- 100 businesses × $300/mo = $360K/year
- 200 businesses × $300/mo = $720K/year

**Pros:**
- ✅ Massive scalability (1,000+ customers possible)
- ✅ Recurring revenue (monthly subscriptions)
- ✅ One codebase serves all customers
- ✅ Exit opportunity (sell to PE/acquirer for 3-5x ARR)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Requires significant upfront development (6-12 months)
- ❌ Need customer support team
- ❌ Churn is a major risk
- ❌ Competing against established platforms (HubSpot, GoHighLevel, etc.)
- ❌ No revenue for 6-12 months while building

**Time Required:**
- Development: 6-12 months full-time
- Customer acquisition: 12-24 months to reach 100 customers
- Ongoing: Support team, development team

**Scalability:** VERY HIGH (but delayed, risky)

---

## Business Model Option 4: "Productized Service" (Hybrid)

**What you sell:**
- Pre-built templates for 3-5 industries (HVAC, Moving, Plumbing, Electrical, Landscaping)
- Fixed-scope setup ($5K-8K)
- Train client to self-manage
- Optional: $200/mo "support plan" (you fix issues, they manage day-to-day)

**Revenue Model:**
- 15 clients/year × $6K = $90K (one-time)
- 10 clients × $200/mo support = $24K/year
- **Year 1: $114K** | **Year 2: $114K** (new clients + growing support base)

**Pros:**
- ✅ Faster delivery (templates = less custom work)
- ✅ Some recurring revenue (support plans)
- ✅ Scalable (can do 3-4 projects/month with templates)
- ✅ Less ongoing management than Model 1

**Cons:**
- ❌ Lower revenue per client (less customization = lower price)
- ❌ Still need constant new clients
- ❌ Support plan churn

**Time Required:**
- Template development: 40-60 hrs upfront (one-time)
- Per client: 10-15 hrs (vs 20-30 for custom)
- **15 clients/year = 150-225 hrs/year = 3-5 hrs/week**

**Scalability:** HIGH (templates enable faster delivery)

## Business Model Option 5: "Voice AI Only" (Niche Focus)

**What you sell:**
- ONLY the Voice AI phone system (not website, not CRM)
- Deep expertise in one thing
- $3K-5K setup + $150-300/mo

**Revenue Model:**
- 20 clients/year × $4K = $80K (one-time)
- 20 clients × $200/mo = $48K/year
- **Year 1: $128K** | **Year 2: $128K**

**Pros:**
- ✅ Clear niche (easier to market: "We do Voice AI for home services")
- ✅ Less scope creep (not managing entire business)
- ✅ Faster delivery (1 week vs 4 weeks)
- ✅ Can partner with web designers (they send you clients)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Lower revenue per client
- ❌ Competing against answering services
- ❌ Smaller total addressable market

**Time Required:**
- Setup: 8-12 hrs per client
- Monthly: 1-2 hrs per client
- **20 clients = 20-40 hrs/month**

**Scalability:** MEDIUM (easier delivery but lower revenue)

---

## Business Model Option 6: "Agency Services" (Client Acquisition Focus)

**What you sell:**
- Lead generation + SMS campaigns + sales support
- You DON'T build infrastructure (website, CRM)
- You fill their pipeline with qualified leads
- $1K-2K/mo retainer per client

**Revenue Model:**
- 10 clients × $1.5K/mo × 12 = $180K/year

**Pros:**
- ✅ No custom development per client (scalable campaigns)
- ✅ Recurring revenue
- ✅ Clear ROI for client (leads = revenue)
- ✅ Can run campaigns for multiple clients simultaneously

**Cons:**
- ❌ Performance risk (if leads don't convert, client churns)
- ❌ Highly competitive space (marketing agencies)
- ❌ Client churn can be high

**Time Required:**
- Campaign setup: 5-8 hrs per client
- Monthly management: 3-5 hrs per client
- **10 clients = 30-50 hrs/month**

**Scalability:** HIGH (campaign-based work is more scalable than custom dev)

## Recommended: Hybrid Approach (Model 2 + Model 5)

**What you actually do:**

### Tier 1: "Website + Voice AI Package" ($10K one-time)
- Build website + Voice AI + basic CRM setup
- Train them to manage it
- Hand it off (no ongoing management)
- **Target:** 12 clients/year = $120K

### Tier 2: "Voice AI Only" ($4K setup + $200/mo)
- For businesses that already have websites
- Just add Voice AI phone system
- **Target:** 8 clients/year = $32K + $19.2K recurring

### Tier 3: "Support Plan" (Optional, $300/mo)
- For clients who want ongoing help
- You fix issues, optimize, add features
- **Target:** 5 clients take this = $18K/year

**Total Revenue:**
- Year 1: $120K + $32K + $19.2K + $18K = **$189K**
- Year 2: $120K + $32K + $37.2K (growing support) = **$189K+**

**Time Required:**
- 20 clients/year × 15 hrs avg = 300 hrs/year = **6 hrs/week**
- Support plan clients: 5 hrs/week
- **Total: ~11 hrs/week**

**Why this works:**
- ✅ Higher revenue per client ($10K vs $8K)
- ✅ Less ongoing burden (handoff model)
- ✅ Two tiers = more customers (some want full build, some just Voice AI)
- ✅ Optional support plan = recurring revenue without being "their tech team"
- ✅ Scalable (can do 2-3 projects/month)

## Questions to Decide Your Model

**1. Do you WANT recurring revenue?**
- YES → Model 1, 5, or 6 (managed services)
- NO → Model 2 (build & handoff)

**2. Do you WANT to manage client operations ongoing?**
- YES → Model 1 (full managed service)
- NO → Model 2 or 4 (handoff models)

**3. How much time do you want to spend per client per month?**
- 0-2 hrs → Model 2 (handoff)
- 3-5 hrs → Model 4 (productized)
- 5-10 hrs → Model 1 or 5 (managed)

**4. What's your revenue goal for Year 1?**
- $100K-150K → Model 4 or 5
- $150K-250K → Model 2 or 6
- $250K+ → Model 3 (SaaS, but delayed)

**5. What do you ENJOY doing most?**
- Building new systems → Model 2 or 4
- Optimizing/managing existing → Model 1
- Marketing/campaigns → Model 6
- Product development → Model 3

## My Recommendation: Start with Model 2 (Build & Handoff)

**Why:**
1. **Higher revenue** ($10K-15K per client vs $8K)
2. **No ongoing management burden** (you're not "their tech team")
3. **Scalable** (can do 2-3/month = $20K-30K/mo)
4. **Clean client relationships** (clear start/end, not stuck supporting forever)
5. **You can always ADD support plans later** ($300/mo for clients who want help)

**What changes from current model:**
- ❌ You DON'T manage their CRM, leads, marketing ongoing
- ❌ You DON'T track their customers
- ✅ You BUILD the system, TRAIN them, HAND IT OFF
- ✅ Optional: They pay $300/mo if they want ongoing support

**Positioning:**
"We build your complete business infrastructure - website, Voice AI, CRM - then train your team to run it. You own it, you manage it, no monthly fees (unless you want support)."

**Pricing:**
- Setup: $10K-15K (one-time, includes training)
- Optional support: $300/mo (only if they want ongoing help)

**Target: 15-20 clients/year = $150K-300K** with NO ongoing management burden.
