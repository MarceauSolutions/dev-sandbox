## Key Decisions Made

### 1. **Business Model: Agency-First, Then Productize**

**NOT doing:** Sell AI assistants as SaaS products ($19-99/month)
- Market saturated (16,955+ MCPs already exist)
- Need 100-200 customers to hit $10K/month
- 77% annual churn for products <$50/month
- 6-12 months to revenue (if successful)

**DOING:** Vertically integrated website + automation (agency services)
- Need 2-4 clients to hit $10K/month
- 3-6 months to $10K/month (more predictable)
- Niche specialists charge 40-60% premium
- Then productize common workflows in Phase 2

---

### 2. **Parallel POCs: HVAC + Shipping (30 Days)**

**Why both?**
- 2x learning speed (30 days vs 60 days sequential)
- Data-driven decision (measure, don't guess)
- Low incremental cost ($20-30 total)
- Same tech stack (tools work for both)

**POC #1: Sr.'s HVAC Business**
- Install Voice AI on business line
- Track calls answered, appointments booked, revenue recovered
- Target: $8K-20K revenue in 30 days
- **Success = compelling case study for other HVAC companies**

**POC #2: William George's Shipping Business**
- Set up lead gen automation (scrape e-commerce sellers)
- Create quote automation system
- Target: 2-5 deals closed, $5K+ revenue in 30 days
- **Success = compelling case study for other shipping brokers**

**After 30 days:** Compare results side-by-side, pick winner (or continue both!)

---

### 3. **3-Phase Roadmap**

**Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Agency Services
- Focus on winner from POC (HVAC or Shipping or both)
- Pricing: $15K implementation + $3K-5K/month retainer
- Goal: $10K-15K/month from 3-4 clients

**Phase 2 (Months 7-12):** Productize Common Workflows
- Extract what ALL clients need into SaaS products
- Example: "HVAC Call Commander" at $299/month
- Goal: $5K-10K/month from 20-30 SaaS customers (additional to agency)

**Phase 3 (Year 2):** Scale + Pursue Validated Fun Ideas
- Multiple revenue streams: Agency + SaaS
- Goal: $50K+/month total
- **Financial freedom to build Elderly Service, Craving Predictor, etc.**

---

### 4. **Fun Ideas = PAUSED Until Cash Flow**

**Deferred to Phase 2-3:**
- Personal Assistant (meta AI assistant)
- Fitness Influencer AI
- Interview Prep AI
- Email Analyzer
- Elderly Person Service
- Craving Predictor
- Claude Framework Community

**Why pause?**
- Need revenue first (make money → fund fun ideas)
- Build ONLY what clients validate demand for
- Avoid building on speculation (77% of products fail)

- **Lesson:** Don't sell just websites, sell outcomes (revenue recovery, time saved)

- **Consumer AI products (<$50/mo) fail:** 77% churn makes unit economics impossible
- **B2B pricing works:** $199-499/month has sustainable retention
- **Niche down ruthlessly:** Specialists charge 40-60% premium vs generalists
- **Validate before building:** Build what clients pay for, not speculation
