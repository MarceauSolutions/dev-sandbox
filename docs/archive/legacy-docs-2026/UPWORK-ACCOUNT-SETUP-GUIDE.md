## Job Search Filters

### Recommended Search Terms
- "automation python"
- "analytics dashboard"
- "API integration"
- "data pipeline"
- "voice AI"
- "Twilio developer"
- "social media automation"
- "AI assistant"

### Job Quality Filters
- Payment verified: Yes
- Spent to date: $1,000+ (avoid new/risky clients)
- Job success: 80%+ (if visible)
- Hire rate: Look for clients who actually hire

### Red Flags to Avoid
- "Need it done ASAP" without clear scope
- Extremely low budgets for complex work
- No payment history
- Vague requirements
- "Looking for long-term partner" in one-off job
