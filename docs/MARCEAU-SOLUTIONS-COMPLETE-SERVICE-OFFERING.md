## Sales Process

### Step 1: Lead Generation

**Methods:**
1. **Warm referrals** (ask William Marceau Sr. + William George for intros)
2. **Cold outreach** (SMS to 100-200 businesses/month)
3. **Content marketing** (blog posts, LinkedIn, local SEO)
4. **Networking** (Chamber, BNI, industry events)

**Template (SMS):**
"Hi [Name], William from Marceau Solutions. I built my dad's HVAC automation system - it now answers calls 24/7 and auto-books appointments. Would a 5-min call to see if this could work for [Business] be helpful?"

---

### Step 2: Discovery Call (20-30 min)

**Goal:** Qualify client, identify pain points, recommend tier

**Questions:**
1. How many locations?
2. How many employees?
3. What's your main pain point? (missing calls, slow follow-up, manual booking)
4. What's your monthly revenue?
5. Do you have internal tech/marketing staff?
6. What's your timeline?

**Outcome:** Recommend tier (Starter, Growth, or Enterprise)

---

### Step 3: Proposal (Send within 24 hours)

**Contents:**
- Tier recommendation with pricing
- What's included (deliverables)
- Timeline (delivery date)
- ROI projection (custom to their business)
- Retainer options (Maintenance vs Partner)
- Case study (HVAC or Shipping, whichever is more relevant)

---

### Step 4: Close & Contract

**Payment Options:**
- Full payment upfront (Stripe invoice)
- 50% deposit + 50% on completion
- Payment plan (for Enterprise: $5K/mo for 3 months)

**Contract:** SOW with deliverables, timeline, support terms

---

### Step 5: Delivery

**Week 1:** Discovery & planning (gather requirements, access to systems)
**Week 2-3:** Build (website, Voice AI, CRM, automations)
**Week 4:** Testing & training (client walkthrough, documentation)
**Week 5:** Go-live & handoff

---

### Step 6: Retainer Upsell (After 30 days)

**Check-in call:**
- How's it working?
- Any issues?
- Want ongoing support? (present Maintenance vs Partner)

**Conversion Rate:** Aim for 50% of clients take a retainer
