## CRM Strategy: Client Businesses vs Your Leads

### Current Setup
**Your ClickUp:**
- Main Leads List (901709132478) - Marceau Solutions prospects
- Client Leads Folder:
  - SW Florida Comfort HVAC Leads (901709854724) - Their customers
  - Square Foot Shipping Leads (901709854725) - Their customers

### Recommended Workflow

#### For YOUR Business (Marceau Solutions):
1. **Lead comes in** via contact form → Goes to Main Leads List
2. **Qualified** → Move to "Active Deals"
3. **Converted** → Move to "Clients" list
4. **Ongoing** → Manage their project in ClickUp

#### For CLIENT Businesses (HVAC, Square Foot):
1. **Their lead comes in** → Goes to THEIR ClickUp list (you see notification)
2. **You manage it for them** → Update status, add notes
3. **Weekly report** → Send them summary of leads + follow-ups
4. **They close the deal** → Mark as won (you get performance data)

### Future Consideration: White-Label CRM?
If you manage leads for multiple clients, you could:
- Offer "Lead Management as a Service" ($X/month)
- They get access to their ClickUp list (view-only)
- You handle all follow-up automation
- Revenue model: Retainer ($500-1000/month) or % of closed deals

### Risk Mitigation

**What if outreach doesn't work?**
- Pivot to referrals (ask HVAC client for intros)
- Join local business groups (Chamber, BNI)
- Partner with web developers (they have restaurant clients)

**What if close rate is low?**
- Lower price point ($3K-5K) to reduce barrier
- Offer free pilot (1 month trial, then convert)
- Add financing option ($1K/mo for 6 months)

**What if delivery is too time-consuming?**
- Hire freelancer for Twilio setup ($500/project)
- Create templated voice flows (reuse across clients)
- Build admin dashboard to reduce support time
