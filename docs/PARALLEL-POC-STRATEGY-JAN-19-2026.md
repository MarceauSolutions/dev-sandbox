# Parallel Proof of Concept Strategy - HVAC + Shipping

**Created:** 2026-01-19
**Duration:** 30 days (Jan 19 - Feb 18, 2026)
**Goal:** Test BOTH verticals simultaneously, pick winner based on results
**Cost:** $20-30 total

---

## Why Run Both POCs in Parallel?

**Advantages:**
1. **2x learning speed** - See which vertical works better in 30 days instead of 60
2. **Data-driven decision** - Don't guess which vertical to focus on, LET THE DATA DECIDE
3. **Low incremental cost** - Both use same tech stack (Voice AI, CRM, automation)
4. **Existing relationships** - Both Sr. (HVAC) and William George (Shipping) are warm connections
5. **Risk mitigation** - If one POC fails, you have the other

**Trade-offs:**
- Slightly more work (but manageable - both are setup projects)
- Need to track 2 case studies instead of 1
- But: **WORTH IT to avoid picking wrong vertical**

---

## POC #1: Sr.'s HVAC Business (SW Florida Comfort)

### What We're Testing
**Hypothesis:** HVAC companies lose $50K-$100K/year from missed calls. Voice AI can recover 80%+ of those calls.

### Setup (Week 1)
- [ ] Install Voice AI on Sr.'s business line
- [ ] Configure HVAC-specific prompts (emergency dispatch, routine maintenance, new customers)
- [ ] Set up call tracking (date, type, AI success, appointments booked, revenue)

### Metrics to Track (Weeks 2-4)
- Total calls received
- AI answer rate (target: >90%)
- Appointments booked (target: 5-10/week)
- Revenue recovered (target: $2K-5K/week)
- Customer satisfaction (any complaints?)

### Success Criteria (End of 30 Days)
- ✅ AI answers 90%+ of calls
- ✅ 15+ appointments booked
- ✅ $8K-20K revenue recovered
- ✅ Sr. reports positive customer feedback
- ✅ Compelling case study for other HVAC companies

## POC #2: William George's Shipping Business (Square Foot Shipping)

### What We're Testing
**Hypothesis:** Shipping brokers need lead generation + quote automation. AI can handle 80%+ of inbound quote requests.

### Setup (Week 1)
- [ ] Set up lead gen system (scrape e-commerce sellers needing shipping)
- [ ] Create quote request form (website or simple landing page)
- [ ] Configure Voice AI for shipping quotes (or use form-based automation)
- [ ] Set up lead tracking (source, quote requested, quote delivered, deal closed)

### Metrics to Track (Weeks 2-4)
- Leads generated (target: 50-100)
- Quote requests received (target: 10-20)
- Quotes delivered (target: 8-15)
- Deals closed (target: 2-5)
- Revenue generated (target: $2K-10K)

### Success Criteria (End of 30 Days)
- ✅ 50+ qualified leads generated
- ✅ 10+ quote requests handled
- ✅ 2+ deals closed
- ✅ William George reports system saves him 10+ hours/week
- ✅ Compelling case study for other shipping brokers

- **Target:** 20+ total quote requests, 3-4 deals closed

**Decision Time:**

**Total Time:** 10 hours

## Decision Matrix (End of 30 Days)

### Scenario 1: HVAC Wins
**If HVAC generates more revenue, easier sales, better margins:**
- Focus 100% on HVAC for Phase 1 (Months 2-6)
- Pause Shipping (or hand off to William George to run independently)
- Use HVAC case study to target 100+ Naples HVAC companies

### Scenario 2: Shipping Wins
**If Shipping generates more revenue, easier sales, better scalability:**
- Focus 100% on Shipping for Phase 1
- Pause HVAC (Sr. keeps the Voice AI, we stop targeting other HVAC companies)
- Use Shipping case study to target shipping brokers nationwide

### Scenario 3: Both Work (Best Case!)
**If BOTH generate revenue and are sustainable:**
- Continue BOTH in Phase 1 (hybrid approach)
- Allocate 50% time to HVAC, 50% to Shipping
- Target: 2 HVAC clients + 2 Shipping clients = $12K-20K/month by Month 6

### Scenario 4: Neither Works (Unlikely)
**If neither POC shows traction:**
- Pivot: Try different verticals (gyms, restaurants, medical)
- OR: Pivot to productized SaaS instead of agency
- Total sunk cost: $15-25 and 60 hours (cheap lesson!)
