## Program Overview

**Duration**: 30 days (4 weeks)
**Training Days**: 4-5 per week (based on energy management system)
**Equipment**: Dumbbells, resistance bands, bench. Gym preferred but home-adaptable.
**Level**: Beginner to intermediate (all exercises have 3 regression levels)
**Target Audience**: People with chronic conditions, injuries, or neurological challenges who want structured training.

---

## Week 1: Foundation

**Goal**: Establish movement baselines, learn the energy management system, build training habits.

### Day 1 — Movement Assessment & Baseline Testing
- Energy rating (1-10) — teach the system from Day 1
- Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min): Physiological sighs (2 min) + Full-body CARs circuit
- **Assessment exercises** (record all numbers):
  - Bodyweight squat: max reps with perfect form
  - Single-leg balance: time (seconds) each side
  - Push-up: max reps (any variation)
  - Plank: max hold time
  - Overhead reach: range of motion (video yourself)
- Cool down: 5 min walking + breathing

### Day 2 — Lower Body Foundation
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Goblet Squat**: 3x10 — Coaching cue: "Chest up, spread the floor with your feet"
  - Regression: Bodyweight squat to box | Progression: Front-loaded split squat
- **A2. Single-Leg RDL (light DB)**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Hinge at the hip, reach for the floor"
  - Regression: Kickstand RDL | Progression: Full single-leg RDL heavy
- **B1. Walking Lunge**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Control the descent, drive through front heel"
  - Regression: Stationary split squat | Progression: Reverse lunge to balance
- **B2. Glute Bridge**: 3x12 — Coaching cue: "Squeeze glutes at the top for 2 seconds"
  - Regression: Feet elevated bridge | Progression: Single-leg glute bridge
- **C. Farmer's Carry**: 3x40 yards — Coaching cue: "Tall posture, squeeze the handles"
- Cool down: 5 min mobility flow

### Day 3 — Active Recovery / Low Day
- Energy rating
- If energy 4+: Full CARs circuit (15 min) + 20 min walk
- If energy 1-3: Breathing drills only (10 min) + gentle stretching
- Journaling prompt: "What did I notice about my body today?"

### Day 4 — Upper Body Foundation
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. DB Bench Press**: 3x10 — Coaching cue: "Lower to chest level, press straight up"
  - Regression: Floor press | Progression: Single-arm DB press
- **A2. Single-Arm DB Row**: 3x10/side — Coaching cue: "Pull to hip, squeeze shoulder blade"
  - Regression: Band row | Progression: Meadows row
- **B1. DB Overhead Press**: 3x8 — Coaching cue: "Brace core, press straight overhead"
  - Regression: Landmine press | Progression: Single-arm OH press
- **B2. Band Pull-Apart**: 3x15 — Coaching cue: "Squeeze shoulder blades together at end range"
- **C1. DB Curl**: 2x12 — Coaching cue: "Control the negative, no swinging"
- **C2. DB Skull Crusher**: 2x12 — Coaching cue: "Elbows stay pointed at ceiling"
- Cool down: 5 min upper body stretching

### Day 5 — Full Body / Conditioning
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **Circuit (3 rounds, 45 sec work / 15 sec rest)**:
  - Goblet squat
  - Push-up (any variation)
  - DB row
  - Glute bridge
  - Farmer's carry (in place if needed)
  - Plank hold
- Rest 2 min between rounds
- Cool down: 5 min walking + breathing

### Days 6-7 — Rest
- Full rest or light walking
- Review Week 1 notes. Adjust energy ratings if needed.

## Week 2: Build

**Goal**: Introduce athletic movements, establish consistent training rhythm, begin complexity progression.

### Day 1 — Lower Body Build
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Bulgarian Split Squat**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Rear foot on bench, torso upright, control the descent"
  - Regression: Supported split squat (hand on rack) | Progression: DB loaded Bulgarian
- **A2. Single-Leg Glute Bridge**: 3x10/side — Coaching cue: "Drive through heel, don't rotate hips"
- **B1. Step-Up (moderate height)**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Drive through top foot only, no push from bottom"
  - Regression: Low box step-up | Progression: Loaded step-up
- **B2. Banded Lateral Walk**: 3x12/side — Coaching cue: "Stay low, don't let knees cave"
- **C. Suitcase Carry**: 3x30 yards/side — Coaching cue: "Don't lean away from the weight"
- Cool down: Hip mobility flow (5 min)

### Day 2 — Upper Body Build
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Single-Arm DB Bench Press**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Brace core to prevent rotation"
- **A2. Half-Kneeling Cable Row** (or band): 3x10/side — Coaching cue: "Stay tall in half-kneeling"
- **B1. Landmine Press**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Full extension, press at 45 degrees"
  - Regression: DB overhead press | Progression: Kneeling landmine press
- **B2. Face Pull** (band or cable): 3x15 — Coaching cue: "Pull to ears, externally rotate at end"
- **C1. Hammer Curl**: 2x10 — Tempo: 3 sec eccentric
- **C2. Tricep Pushdown**: 2x12 — Coaching cue: "Elbows pinned to sides"
- Cool down: Shoulder CARs (3 min)

### Day 3 — Active Recovery
- Full CARs circuit (15 min)
- 20-30 min zone 2 cardio (walk, bike, swim — whatever feels best)
- Journaling prompt: "Am I recovering well? What's my average energy this week?"

### Day 4 — Full Body Athletic
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Goblet Squat to Press**: 3x8 — Coaching cue: "Squat deep, explode up, press at the top"
- **A2. Renegade Row** (or modified from knees): 3x6/side — Coaching cue: "Wide feet for stability"
  - Regression: Plank hold + alternating row | Progression: Full renegade row with push-up
- **B1. Reverse Lunge to Balance**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Step back, drive up, hold balance for 2 sec"
- **B2. Push-Up** (any variation): 3x max reps — Coaching cue: "Stop 1-2 reps before form breaks down"
- **C. Conditioning**: 8 min EMOM — Odd minutes: 10 KB/DB swings | Even minutes: 5 burpees (modified OK)
- Cool down: Full body stretch (5 min)

### Day 5 — Optional Bonus / Mobility
- If energy is high: Repeat any Day 1-4 workout with 10% more load
- If energy is low: Extended mobility session (30 min CARs + foam rolling)

### Days 6-7 — Rest

## Week 3: Challenge

**Goal**: Increase movement complexity, add conditioning volume, push boundaries safely.

### Day 1 — Lower Body Challenge
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Rear-Foot-Elevated Split Squat** (loaded): 4x6/side — Tempo: 3 sec eccentric
- **A2. Single-Leg RDL** (moderate load): 3x8/side
- **B1. Lateral Lunge**: 3x8/side — Coaching cue: "Push hips back, load the working leg"
  - Regression: Bodyweight lateral squat | Progression: DB loaded lateral lunge
- **B2. Copenhagen Plank**: 3x20 sec/side — Coaching cue: "Top leg on bench, body straight"
  - Regression: Bent knee Copenhagen | Progression: Full Copenhagen with movement
- **C. Conditioning**: 4 rounds — 200m row or 30 sec bike sprint + 10 goblet squats + 60 sec rest
- Cool down: Hip opener flow (5 min)

### Day 2 — Upper Body Challenge
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Floor Press** (heavy): 4x6 — Coaching cue: "Pause at the bottom, elbows touch floor"
- **A2. Chest-Supported Row**: 4x8 — Coaching cue: "Squeeze at the top for 1 sec"
- **B1. Single-Arm Overhead Press**: 3x6/side — Coaching cue: "Brace hard, press with control"
- **B2. Prone Y-T-W Raises**: 3x8 each position — Coaching cue: "Light weight, full range"
- **C1. Incline Curl**: 3x10 — Tempo: 2 sec eccentric
- **C2. Overhead Tricep Extension**: 3x10
- Cool down: Thoracic spine mobility (5 min)

### Day 3 — Conditioning + Core
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **Conditioning Circuit (4 rounds)**:
  - 30 sec work / 15 sec rest per station
  - Station 1: DB thruster
  - Station 2: Mountain climber (controlled)
  - Station 3: KB/DB swing
  - Station 4: Plank shoulder tap
  - Station 5: Jump squat (or squat to calf raise if jumping is contraindicated)
  - Rest 90 sec between rounds
- **Core finisher**: 3 rounds — Dead bug x10/side + Pallof press x10/side + Side plank x20 sec/side
- Cool down: Full body stretch

### Day 4 — Full Body Strength
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Trap Bar Deadlift or DB Sumo Deadlift**: 4x6 — Coaching cue: "Push the floor away"
  - Regression: Elevated deadlift | Progression: Conventional deadlift
- **A2. Push-Up Variation** (pike, diamond, or archer): 3x8-10
- **B1. Walking Lunge** (loaded): 3x8/side
- **B2. Cable or Band Pulldown**: 3x12
- **C. Loaded Carry Complex**: 3 rounds — Farmer's carry 20 yds → Overhead carry 20 yds → Suitcase carry 20 yds/side
- Cool down: Walking + breathing (5 min)

### Day 5 — Active Recovery
- Extended CARs + mobility (20-30 min)
- Zone 2 cardio (20 min)
- Journaling prompt: "What exercise improved the most this week? What still feels limited?"

### Days 6-7 — Rest

## Week 4: Peak + Transition

**Goal**: Re-test baselines, celebrate progress, plan the next phase.

### Day 1 — Lower Body Peak
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. Bulgarian Split Squat**: Work up to a challenging 3x5/side (heavier than Week 2)
- **A2. Single-Leg RDL**: Work up to a challenging 3x6/side
- **B1. Box Jump or Box Step-Up** (explosive): 3x5 — Coaching cue: "Land soft, step down"
  - Regression: Squat to calf raise | Progression: Depth jump
- **B2. Banded Monster Walk**: 3x10/direction
- **C. Finisher**: Tabata goblet squats (20 on/10 off x 8 rounds)
- Cool down: Full lower body stretch

### Day 2 — Upper Body Peak
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **A1. DB Bench Press**: Work up to a challenging 3x5 (heaviest of the program)
- **A2. Barbell or DB Row**: Work up to a challenging 3x6
- **B1. Overhead Press**: Work up to a challenging 3x5
- **B2. Pull-Up or Lat Pulldown**: 3x max reps (or 3x8 weighted)
- **C. Arm superset**: 3 rounds — Curl x10 + Dip or pushdown x10 — no rest
- Cool down: Upper body stretch + shoulder CARs

### Day 3 — Re-Test Day (IMPORTANT)
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- **Re-test all Day 1 assessments**:
  - Bodyweight squat: max reps
  - Single-leg balance: time each side
  - Push-up: max reps
  - Plank: max hold
  - Overhead reach: range of motion (video)
- **Record everything. Compare to Week 1.**
- Celebrate improvement. Any progress is progress.
- Cool down: Full body stretch

### Day 4 — Final Full Body Session
- Warm-up: Nervous System Reset Protocol (10 min)
- Pick your FAVORITE exercise from each category and do 4 sets of your best:
  - Squat pattern (any variation)
  - Hinge pattern (any variation)
  - Push pattern (any variation)
  - Pull pattern (any variation)
  - Carry pattern (any variation)
  - Core (any variation)
- This is your victory lap. Train hard, train proud.
- Cool down: Extended stretch + breathing (10 min)

### Day 5 — Active Recovery + Planning
- Full CARs circuit
- 20 min easy cardio
- **Plan your next phase**:
  - What worked best? Do more of it.
  - What needs work? Prioritize it.
  - Energy patterns: What does your 4-week data show?
  - Ready for coaching? Book the free strategy call.

### Days 6-7 — Rest & Reflect

## Program Principles (Reference Card)

### Energy Management System
| Rating | Day Type | Training Approach |
|--------|----------|-------------------|
| 7-10 | High Day | Full training. Heavy loads, full volume. Push it. |
| 4-6 | Medium Day | Modified. Reduce load 20%, cut 1-2 sets/exercise. Focus technique. |
| 1-3 | Low Day | Active recovery. Mobility, CARs, walking, breathing. No ego. |

### Nervous System Reset Protocol (Every Session)
1. Physiological sighs: 2 minutes (double inhale nose, long exhale mouth)
2. Full-body CARs: Neck → Shoulders → Spine → Hips → Ankles (90 sec each)
3. Total: 10-12 minutes

### Progressive Overload Rules
- Week 1-2: Master the movement pattern at moderate load
- Week 3: Add 1 complexity layer OR 5-10% more load
- Week 4: Test your peak. New baselines.
- Never sacrifice form for load. Complexity > weight.

### Nutrition Basics
- Protein: 0.7-1g per pound bodyweight daily
- Hydration: Half bodyweight in ounces of water
- Anti-inflammatory focus: omega-3s, colorful vegetables, minimize processed food
- Pre-workout: Protein + carbs 60-90 min before training
- Post-workout: Protein within 60 min of training
