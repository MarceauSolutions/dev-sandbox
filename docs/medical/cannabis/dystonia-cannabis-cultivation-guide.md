## 1. Legal Considerations (Florida)

### Current Status (2026)
- **Medical patients CAN grow** — Florida legalized home cultivation for MMJ patients
- **Plant limits:** Up to 6 mature plants per household (12 if multiple patients)
- **Must be secured:** Locked space, not visible from public
- **No sales:** Personal use only
- **Keep your card current:** Must maintain active MMJ patient status

### Requirements
- Valid Florida Medical Marijuana card
- Plants must be grown indoors or in locked greenhouse
- Cannot be visible or accessible to public
- Must be in your primary residence

## 5. Germination

### Paper Towel Method (Most Reliable)

**Materials:**
- Seeds
- Paper towels (unbleached preferred)
- Two plates or tupperware
- Distilled or pH-balanced water (6.0-6.5)

**Steps:**

1. **Soak seeds** (optional) — Drop seeds in glass of distilled water for 12-24 hours. Viable seeds sink.

2. **Prepare paper towels:**
   - Wet paper towel until damp (not dripping)
   - Place on plate
   - Put seeds on towel, 1 inch apart
   - Cover with another damp paper towel
   - Cover with second plate (dome)

3. **Store in dark, warm place:**
   - Temperature: 70-85°F (ideal: 78°F)
   - Check every 12 hours, keep moist
   - Don't let dry out

4. **Wait for taproot:**
   - Most seeds crack in 24-72 hours
   - Wait until taproot is ¼" to ½" long
   - Handle with tweezers, don't touch taproot

5. **Plant:**
   - Pre-moisten soil
   - Make ¼" hole with pencil
   - Place seed taproot DOWN
   - Lightly cover with soil
   - Mist surface, don't soak

**Timeline:** 2-7 days from seed to sprout above soil

## 6. Vegetative Stage

### Environment

| Factor | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Light schedule | 18 hours on / 6 hours off |
| Temperature | 70-85°F (ideal: 77°F) |
| Humidity | 50-70% (higher for seedlings) |
| pH (soil) | 6.0-7.0 (ideal: 6.5) |
| pH (hydro) | 5.5-6.5 (ideal: 5.8) |

### Week-by-Week Guide

**Week 1-2 (Seedling):**
- Light: 24" above canopy, dimmed to 50%
- Water: Mist only, keep soil moist not wet
- Nutrients: None (soil has enough)
- Humidity: 65-70%

**Week 3-4:**
- Light: 18-24" above, 75% power
- Water: When top inch of soil is dry
- Nutrients: Start at ¼ strength
- Begin light training (bend stem gently)

**Week 5-6:**
- Light: Full power
- Nutrients: ½ strength, increase nitrogen
- Top the plant (cut above 5th node) to encourage bushiness
- Low Stress Training (LST): Tie branches down to spread canopy

**Week 7-8:**
- Nutrients: Full strength veg formula
- Continue training
- Watch for sex (remove any males immediately)

### Feeding Schedule (Fox Farm Trio)

| Week | Big Bloom | Grow Big | Tiger Bloom |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| 1-2 | — | — | — |
| 3 | 1 tsp/gal | ½ tsp/gal | — |
| 4 | 2 tsp/gal | 1 tsp/gal | — |
| 5-6 | 2 tsp/gal | 2 tsp/gal | — |
| 7-8 | 2 tsp/gal | 3 tsp/gal | — |

**Water pH:** Always check and adjust to 6.3-6.8 before feeding.

### When to Flip to Flower

- Plant has 6-8 nodes
- Filled 50-60% of tent space (will double in flower)
- Usually 4-8 weeks of veg
- You control timing by changing light schedule

## 7. Flowering Stage

### Triggering Flower

**Change light schedule to 12/12** (12 hours on, 12 hours off)

⚠️ **Critical:** Complete darkness during "off" period. Any light leaks can cause stress/hermaphroditism.

### Environment

| Factor | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Light schedule | 12 on / 12 off |
| Temperature | 65-80°F |
| Humidity | 40-50% (lower to prevent mold) |
| Airflow | Constant, gentle breeze |

### Week-by-Week Guide

**Week 1-2 (Transition/Stretch):**
- Plants will stretch 50-100% in height
- Switch nutrients to bloom formula
- Watch for male flowers (remove immediately)
- Continue tucking leaves for light penetration

**Week 3-4:**
- White pistils (hairs) appear
- Buds begin forming at nodes
- Increase bloom nutrients
- Lower humidity to 45-50%

**Week 5-6:**
- Buds fattening
- Trichomes visible (get a jeweler's loupe)
- Aroma intensifies (ensure carbon filter working)
- May need to support heavy branches

**Week 7-8:**
- Peak flowering
- Stop nitrogen, focus on P-K
- Watch trichomes for harvest timing
- Begin flush in final 1-2 weeks

**Week 9-10+ (strain dependent):**
- Flush with plain pH'd water (no nutrients)
- 10-14 days of flushing
- Watch trichomes for harvest timing

### Feeding Schedule (Flower)

| Week | Big Bloom | Grow Big | Tiger Bloom |
|------|-----------|----------|-------------|
| 1-2 | 2 tsp/gal | 1 tsp/gal | 1 tsp/gal |
| 3-4 | 2 tsp/gal | ½ tsp/gal | 2 tsp/gal |
| 5-6 | 2 tsp/gal | — | 3 tsp/gal |
| 7-8 | 2 tsp/gal | — | 3 tsp/gal |
| 9+ | Plain water (flush) | — | — |

## 8. Harvest

### When to Harvest

**Use a jeweler's loupe (60x) or digital microscope to check trichomes:**

| Trichome Color | Effect | When |
|----------------|--------|------|
| Clear | Too early | Wait |
| Cloudy/milky | Peak THC, energetic | Harvest for daytime strains |
| 20-30% amber | More body, sedative | Harvest for pain/sleep strains |
| Majority amber | Very sedating, couch-lock | Good for nighttime dystonia relief |

**For your dystonia:**
- **Daytime strains (ACDC, Harlequin):** Harvest at mostly cloudy
- **Nighttime strains (Northern Lights):** Harvest at 30-40% amber

### How to Harvest

1. **Stop watering 1-2 days before** (helps dry faster)

2. **Cut the plant:**
   - Option A: Cut whole plant at base
   - Option B: Cut individual branches

3. **Trim (wet trim vs dry trim):**
   - **Wet trim:** Remove fan leaves and sugar leaves immediately (easier, faster drying)
   - **Dry trim:** Remove only fan leaves, trim sugar leaves after drying (better flavor, slower)

4. **Save trim:** Sugar leaves have trichomes — save for edibles/hash

## 9. Drying

### Setup

- **Dark room** (light degrades THC)
- **Temperature:** 60-70°F (ideal: 65°F)
- **Humidity:** 55-65% (ideal: 60%)
- **Airflow:** Gentle, not directly on buds
- **Duration:** 7-14 days

### Methods

**Hang Drying (Recommended):**
1. Hang branches upside down on string/hangers
2. Space for airflow between branches
3. Small fan for circulation (not pointed at buds)
4. Check daily

**Rack Drying:**
1. Use mesh drying rack
2. Spread buds in single layer
3. Turn buds every 1-2 days
4. Faster but more hands-on

### When It's Dry

- Small stems snap (not bend)
- Buds feel dry on outside but slightly spongy
- Usually 7-14 days depending on conditions

⚠️ **Don't rush!** Too-fast drying = harsh smoke, hay smell

## 10. Curing

### Why Cure?

- Breaks down chlorophyll (removes "green" taste)
- Develops terpene profile (better flavor/aroma)
- Smooths smoke (less harsh)
- Allows moisture to equalize
- Improves potency (converts THCA to THC)

### How to Cure

1. **Trim** (if you dry-trimmed)

2. **Jar it:**
   - Use mason jars (wide-mouth quart size)
   - Fill 75% full (leave air space)
   - Don't pack tight

3. **Burp daily:**
   - Week 1: Open jars 2-3x daily for 10-15 minutes
   - Week 2: Open once daily for 5-10 minutes
   - Week 3-4: Open every 2-3 days
   - Week 5+: Burp weekly, ready to smoke

4. **Monitor humidity:**
   - Use Boveda 62% packs or small hygrometer
   - Target: 58-65% RH in jar
   - Too wet (>70%): Leave lid off for a few hours
   - Too dry (<55%): Add Boveda pack

### Cure Timeline

| Time | Quality |
|------|---------|
| 2 weeks | Smokeable but not optimal |
| 4 weeks | Good, noticeable improvement |
| 8 weeks | Great, full flavor development |
| 3-6 months | Peak quality for most strains |

**For medical use:** Minimum 4-week cure recommended for smoothest effect.

## 11. Processing for Medical Use

### Options Beyond Smoking

#### 1. RSO (Rick Simpson Oil) — Best for Pain

**What it is:** Full-spectrum, highly concentrated cannabis oil

**Why for dystonia:** Most potent option for severe pain, long-lasting

**How to make:**

*Materials:*
- 1 oz cannabis (flower or trim)
- High-proof alcohol (Everclear) — 1 quart
- Pyrex dish
- Rice cooker or double boiler
- Syringes for storage

*Steps:*
1. Decarboxylate cannabis (240°F for 40 min)
2. Place in jar, cover with alcohol
3. Shake for 3 minutes
4. Strain through cheesecloth into Pyrex
5. Repeat wash 1-2 more times
6. Evaporate alcohol (rice cooker on low, OUTDOORS — fumes flammable)
7. Scrape oil into syringes

**Dose:** Start with rice-grain sized dose, wait 2 hours

#### 2. Cannabis-Infused Coconut Oil

**Good for:** Edibles, capsules, topicals

*Materials:*
- 1 oz decarbed cannabis
- 2 cups coconut oil
- Slow cooker or double boiler

*Steps:*
1. Decarb cannabis (240°F, 40 min)
2. Combine with coconut oil in slow cooker
3. Cook on low 4-6 hours, stir occasionally
4. Strain through cheesecloth
5. Store in jar in fridge

**Use:** Fill capsules, make edibles, or apply topically

#### 3. Topical Balm (For Shoulder/Trap)

**Why:** Direct application to affected muscles

*Ingredients:*
- ½ cup cannabis coconut oil
- 2 tbsp beeswax
- 10 drops peppermint essential oil (optional)
- 10 drops lavender essential oil (optional)

*Steps:*
1. Melt coconut oil and beeswax together
2. Remove from heat, add essential oils
3. Pour into tins/jars
4. Let cool and solidify

**Use:** Apply directly to shoulder, trap, arm as needed

#### 4. Tincture (Sublingual)

**Good for:** Fast onset, precise dosing

*Steps:*
1. Decarb cannabis
2. Place in mason jar
3. Cover with high-proof alcohol
4. Store in dark place 2-4 weeks, shake daily
5. Strain, bottle with dropper

**Use:** Drop under tongue, hold 60 seconds, swallow

## 12. Consumption Methods for Dystonia

### Method Comparison

| Method | Onset | Duration | Best For |
|--------|-------|----------|----------|
| **Smoking** | 1-5 min | 1-3 hours | Immediate relief, breakthrough pain |
| **Vaporizing** | 1-5 min | 1-3 hours | Healthier than smoking, immediate |
| **RSO/Oil** | 30-90 min | 4-8 hours | Long-lasting, nighttime, severe pain |
| **Edibles** | 30-120 min | 4-8 hours | Long-lasting, discreet |
| **Tincture** | 15-45 min | 2-4 hours | Moderate onset, easy dosing |
| **Topical** | 15-30 min (local) | 2-4 hours | Localized muscle relief |

### Recommended Protocol for Your Condition

**Morning (Daytime Function):**
- ACDC or Harlequin (high-CBD)
- Method: Vaporize or tincture
- Dose: Start low, titrate up until pain relieved but function preserved

**Afternoon (Breakthrough Pain):**
- Harlequin or 1:1 strain
- Method: Vaporize as needed
- Keep dose moderate to maintain function

**Evening (Heavy Relief):**
- Northern Lights or indica-dominant
- Method: Smoke/vape + RSO oil
- Can dose more aggressively for sleep

**Topical (As Needed):**
- Apply balm to shoulder/trap
- Can use any time, won't cause impairment
- Layer with other methods

## 13. Dosing Protocol

### Start Low, Go Slow

**For smoking/vaping:**
- Start: 1-2 puffs
- Wait 15 minutes
- Assess, add more if needed
- Find minimum effective dose

**For RSO/edibles:**
- Start: Rice-grain size RSO or 2.5mg THC edible
- Wait 2 hours (full onset)
- Increase by 2.5mg next time if needed
- Effective doses vary: 5-50mg+ depending on tolerance

### Building Tolerance Strategically

- **Microdosing:** 1-2.5mg THC maintains function
- **T-breaks:** 48-72 hours resets tolerance significantly
- **CBD alongside:** Can reduce THC tolerance buildup
- **Rotate strains:** Prevents single-strain tolerance

### Tracking

Keep a log:
- Strain
- Method
- Dose
- Time taken
- Pain level before (1-10)
- Pain level after (1-10)
- Side effects
- Function impact

This helps dial in optimal protocol.
