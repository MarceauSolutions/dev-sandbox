## Month 1 Parallel Campaign Plan

### Campaign 1: Marceau Solutions - Naples Gyms (Priority 1)

**Target:** 600 Naples gym owners
**Channel:** Apollo email sequences
**Budget:** $0 (uses Apollo's 1,200 free emails/month)
**Timeline:** 4 weeks

**Value Proposition:**
"Automate member retention, class scheduling, and lead follow-up. Save 10-20 hours/week."

**Email Sequence (3 emails):**
1. **Day 0:** Pain point intro - "Are you losing members because they can't reach you after hours?"
2. **Day 3:** Social proof - "3 Naples gyms automated their follow-up and saw 15% retention increase"
3. **Day 7:** Direct CTA - "Book a 15-min call to see how we can automate your gym"

**Expected Results:**
- Emails sent: 600 (Apollo)
- Open rate: 25-35% (150-210 opens)
- Reply rate: 2-5% (12-30 replies)
- Discovery calls: 3-5
- Clients closed: 1-2
- **Revenue: $5K-10K** (Digital Storefront @ $4,997 or Growth System @ $9,997)
- **ROI: Infinite** (spent $0)

## Why This Beats Sequential Approach

| **Time to first Marceau client** | 30 days | 60+ days (wait for HVAC results first) |

| **Risk** | Low ($5 total) | Same ($5) |
| **Upside** | High ($12K possible) | Low ($2K max in Month 1) |

**Winner:** Parallel approach - same cost, 6x higher upside, 2x faster learning.
