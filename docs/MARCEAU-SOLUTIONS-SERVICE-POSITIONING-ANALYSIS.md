- Setup: $8K revenue - $5K cost (high estimate) = **$3K profit (38% margin)**

Your monthly fee is too low. At $200/mo, you're subsidizing clients after setup.

**Recommended Pricing Adjustment:**
- Setup: $8K (keep as-is)
- Monthly: **$400-500/mo** (to cover costs + profit)
- OR: Setup: **$10K** (to offset monthly subsidy)
