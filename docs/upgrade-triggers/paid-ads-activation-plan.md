# Paid Ads Activation Plan

## Trigger: MRR > $2,000/mo (client-financed acquisition)

## What This Activates
Meta (Facebook/Instagram) ads targeting Naples FL for both AI services and fitness coaching.

## Why Wait Until $2K MRR
- Hormozi principle: client-financed acquisition. Don't spend money you don't have.
- At $2K MRR, you can allocate $500/mo to ads (25% of revenue) without risk.
- You'll have case studies and testimonials by then (proof for ad creative).

## Activation Steps
1. Create Meta Business Manager account (if not exists)
2. Install Meta Pixel on marceausolutions.com
3. Create 2 campaigns: AI Services (Naples SMBs) + Fitness Coaching (online)
4. Use case study results as ad creative
5. Start at $20/day per campaign, optimize weekly
6. Track via UTM parameters → n8n lead capture → Scorecard Sheet
7. AutoIterator can optimize ad copy once data flows

## Estimated Effort: 1 session (2-3 hours)
