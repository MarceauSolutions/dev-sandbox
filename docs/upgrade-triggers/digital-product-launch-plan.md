# Digital Product Launch Plan — $19.99 Training Program

## Trigger: 10+ YouTube videos published

## What This Launches
"The Defy the Odds Training System" — 30-day self-guided program for people training through health challenges. $19.99 one-time purchase.

## Why Wait For 10 Videos
- Need content library to drive traffic to the product
- Each video's CTA points to the product
- 10 videos = enough SEO surface area to generate organic sales

## Launch Steps
1. Create Stripe product: one-time $19.99
2. Create Gumroad listing as backup sales channel
3. Build delivery automation: Stripe payment → n8n → send PDF + video links via email
4. Product content already outlined: docs/digital-product-outline.md
5. Generate the full 30-day PDF program using workout_plan_generator.py + branded_pdf_engine.py
6. Record 4 instructional videos (one per week of the program)
7. Add purchase link to YouTube descriptions and landing page
8. AutoIterator optimizes the sales page copy
