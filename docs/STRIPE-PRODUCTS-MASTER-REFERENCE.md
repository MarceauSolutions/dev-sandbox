## Pricing Rationale (Why These Numbers)

### Setup Tiers:
- **Starter ($2,997):** ~3x cost of your time (15-20 hrs @ $150/hr = $2,250-3,000)
- **Growth ($7,497):** ~2.5x cost of your time (30-40 hrs @ $150/hr = $4,500-6,000)
- **Enterprise ($14,997):** ~2x cost of your time (60-80 hrs @ $150/hr = $9,000-12,000)

Lower margin on Enterprise = justifiable because retainer attach rate is 90%+

### Retainer Tiers:
- **Maintenance ($747/mo):** 5 hrs @ $150/hr = $750 (breaks even)
- **Partner ($2,247/mo):** 10 hrs @ $150/hr = $1,500 (50% margin)

Higher margin on Partner = reflects strategic value, not just hourly labor

### Community:
- **Monthly ($47/mo):** Digital product, 95% margin after setup
- **Annual ($397/yr):** 30% discount to lock in revenue, reduce churn
