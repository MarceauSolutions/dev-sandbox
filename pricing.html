Frequently Asked Questions

Why bundle website + automation instead of selling separately?
A website without automation is just a digital brochure. 80% of visitors leave without taking action. Our automation captures those leads, follows up automatically, and converts them. That's where the real value is. Plus, most automation (call routing, booking forms, quote requests) requires a website to work properly.
What's the typical ROI?
Example: An HVAC company pays $9,997 for the Growth System. They book 5 extra appointments per month (from calls they used to miss). At $500 average job value = $2,500/month extra revenue. System pays for itself in 4 months. After that, it's pure profit.
Can I just get the website without automation?
We don't offer website-only because a website without automation doesn't solve your real problem: converting visitors into paying customers. Our integrated approach ensures your website actually generates revenue, not just looks pretty.
What if I need more features later?
You have two options: (1) Pay separately for each additional workflow ($1,500-3,000 depending on complexity), or (2) Upgrade to the next tier and pay the difference. For example, if you started with Digital Storefront ($4,997) and want everything in Growth System ($9,997), you'd pay $5,000 to upgrade.
Do I need the retainer after launch?
Digital Storefront and Growth System clients: Retainers are optional after your warranty period. Maintenance Retainer ($747/mo) is recommended if you want peace of mind. Partner Retainer ($2,247/mo) is for businesses that want continuous optimization. Enterprise Package clients: Partner Retainer is REQUIRED for the first 6 months to ensure you get full ROI from your investment.
What industries do you work with?
We specialize in local service businesses: HVAC, plumbing, electrical, moving companies, restaurants, gyms, professional services (lawyers, accountants, consultants). If you serve local customers and take appointments or orders, we can help.
What are the payment terms?
50% deposit to start work, 50% balance due at launch. We accept credit card (Stripe) or ACH bank transfer. Payment plans available for Enterprise Package clients.
